Chapter 1

The advent of Lord Krishna

श्रीमद्भागवतमहापुराणम्

दशमः स्कन्धः

श्रीकृष्णावतारोपक्रमः नाम  प्रथमः अध्यायः

(69 Shlokas)

राजा उवाच

कथितो वंशविस्तारो भवता सोमसूर्ययोः।

राज्ञां चोभयो वंश्यानां चरितं परमाद्भुतम् ॥10.1.1॥

यदोश्च धर्मशीलस्य नितरां मुनिसत्तम ।

Word-by-word meaning: राजा उवाच the king said, कथितः has been described, वंश-विस्तारः expansion of the dynasties, भवता by you, सोम-सूर्ययोः of Soma and Surya (Moon and Sun dynasties), राज्ञाम् of the kings, च and, उभयोः वंश्यानाम् of both of the descendants, चरितम् the deeds (or history), परम अद्भुतम् extremely wonderful, यदोः च and of Yadu, धर्म-शीलस्य of one who is virtuous, नितराम् always, मुनिसत्तम O best of sages

Anvay: राजा उवाच — मुनिसत्तम! भवता सोमसूर्ययोः वंशविस्तारः, उभयोः वंश्यानां राज्ञां च, नितरां धर्मशीलस्य यदोः च परम अद्भुतं चरितं कथितः।

Literal meaning of the Anvaya: राजा उवाच — The King said, मुनिसत्तम! O Best amongst the sages, भवता by you, सोमसूर्ययोः वंशविस्तारः the expansion of the dynasties of the Moon and the Sun, उभयोः वंश्यानां राज्ञां च and of the kings of both the dynasties, नितरां धर्मशीलस्य यदोः च  and of the always virtuous Yadu, परम अद्भुतं चरितं the supremely wonderful deeds, कथितः have been described.

Simple meaning: The king (Pareekshit) said: “O best of sages! The expansion of the dynasties of the Moon and the Sun, and the supremely wonderful deeds of the kings belonging to both these dynasties and of the always virtuous Yadu have been described by you (Shukadeva Gosvami).

तत्रांशेनावतीर्णस्य विष्णोर्वीर्याणि शंस नः॥10.1.2॥

Word-by-word meaning: तत्र there, अंशेन part, अवतीर्णस्य manifested, विष्णोः of Vishnu, वीर्याणि the glorious deeds, शंस tell, नः to us

Anvay: तत्र अंशेन अवतीर्णस्य विष्णोः वीर्याणि नः शंस।  

Literal meaning of the Anvaya: तत्र there, अंशेन अवतीर्णस्य विष्णोः वीर्याणि glorious deeds of Lord Vishnu who manifested in part, नः शंस please describe to us

Simple meaning: “Please describe to us the glorious deeds of Lord Vishnu, who manifested in part there (in the virtuous dynasty of Yadu).

अवतीर्य यदोर्वंशे भगवान् भूतभावनः ।

कृतवान् यानि विश्वात्मा तानि नो वद विस्तरात् ॥10.1.3॥

Word-by-word meaning: अवतीर्य having descended, यदोः वंशे in the dynasty of Yadu, भगवान् the Supreme Lord, भूत-भावनः the protector of all living beings, कृतवान् performed, यानि whatever, विश्वात्मा the Soul of the universe, तानि those, नः to us, वद speak, विस्तरात् in detail

Anvay: भगवान् भूतभावनः विश्वात्मा यदोः वंशे अवतीर्य यानि कृतवान्, तानि विस्तरात् नः वद।

Literal meaning of the Anvaya: भगवान् भूतभावनः विश्वात्मा the Lord, protector of all living beings and The Soul of the universe, यदोः वंशे अवतीर्य having descended in the dynasty of Yadu, यानि कृतवान् whatever deeds He performed, तानि विस्तरात् नः वद those, in detail, please tell us.

Simple meaning: “Whatever deeds, The Supreme Lord, The Protector of all living beings, The Soul of the universe performed after having descended in the dynasty of Yadu, please speak to us in detail about them.

निवृत्ततर्षैरुपगीयमानाद् भवौषधाच्छ्रोत्रमनोऽभिरामात् ।

क उत्तमश्लोकगुणानुवादात् पुमान् विरज्येत विना पशुघ्नात् ॥10.1.4॥

Word-by-word meaning: निवृत्त-तर्षैः by those who are free from thirst (of material desires), उपगीयमानात् being sung, भव-औषधात् the medicine for material existence, श्रोत्र-मनः अभिरामात् pleasing to the ear and to the mind, कः who, उत्तम-श्लोक-गुण-अनुवादात् from the glorification of qualities of the Supreme Lord, पुमान् a person, विरज्येत would turn away, विना except, पशु-घ्नात् except the one who is a killer of animals

Anvay: निवृत्ततर्षैः उपगीयमानात्, भवौषधात्, श्रोत्रमनोऽभिरामात्, उत्तमश्लोकगुणानुवादात् कः पुमान् विरज्येत—विना पशुघ्नात् ?

Literal meaning of the Anvaya: निवृत्ततर्षैः उपगीयमानात् Sung by those free from thirst of material desires, भवौषधात् which act as the medicine for the disease of material existence, श्रोत्रमनोऽभिरामात् which are pleasing to the ear and the mind, कः पुमान् which man, उत्तमश्लोकगुणानुवादात् विरज्येत would turn away from the glories of the Supreme Lord, — विना पशुघ्नात् except one who is a killer of animals?

Simple meaning: “Who would ever turn away from the glories of the Supreme Lord, sung by those free from thirst of material desires, which act as the medicine for the disease of material existence and are pleasing to the ear and the mind—except one who is a killer of animals?

पितामहा मे समरेऽमरञ्जयैर्देवव्रताद्यातिरथैस्तिमिङ्गिलैः ।

दुरत्ययं कौरवसैन्यसागरं कृत्वातरन् वत्सपदं स्म यत्प्लवाः ॥10.1.5॥

Word-by-word meaning: पितामहाः grandfathers, मे my, समरे in battle, अमरम्-जयैः by those who conquered even the demigods, देवव्रत-आद्य-अतिरथैः by great warriors like Devavrata (Bhishma) and the others, तिमिङ्गिलैः compared to giant sea monsters, दुरत्ययम् insurmountable, कौरव-सैन्य-सागरम् ocean of the Kaurava army, कृत्वा making, अतरन् crossed over, वत्स-पदम् like the hoofprint of a calf, स्म indeed, यत् प्लवाः by whom as a means of crossing

Anvay: मे पितामहाः, – तिमिङ्गिलैः (इव)​ – अमरञ्जयैः देवव्रताद्यातिरथैः दुरत्ययं कौरवसैन्यसागरं यत् ​प्लवाः कृत्वा – वत्सपदं (इव) – अतरन् स्म।

Literal meaning of the Anvaya: मे पितामहाः My grandfathers, तिमिङ्गिलैः (इव)​ as dangerous as giant sea monsters, अमरञ्जयैः who conquered even the demigods, देवव्रताद्यातिरथैः great warriors like Devavrata (Bhishma) and the others, दुरत्ययं कौरवसैन्यसागरं insurmountable ocean of the Kaurava army, यत् प्लवाः कृत्वा whom they literally made the raft to cross (i.e., the protection of Lord Krishna is compared to the raft by which the Pandavas crossed the ocean of Kauravas), वत्सपदं (इव) अतरन् स्म as easily as stepping over a calf’s hoof print.

Simple meaning: “My grandfathers crossed the otherwise insurmountable ocean of the Kaurava army, which was filled with great warriors like Devavrata (Bhishma) and the others, who conquered even the demigods —because of the protection of Lord Krishna which was like a raft, as easily as stepping over a calf’s hoofprint.

द्रौण्यस्त्रविप्लुष्टमिदं मदङ्गं सन्तानबीजं कुरुपाण्डवानाम् ।

जुगोप कुक्षिं गत आत्तचक्रो मातुश्च मे यः शरणं गतायाः ॥10.1.6॥

Word-by-word meaning: द्रौणि-अस्त्र-विप्लुष्टम् burned by the weapon of Drauni (Son of Drona - Ashwathama), इदम् this, मत् अङ्गम् my body, सन्तान-बीजम् the seed of continuation, कुरु-पाण्डवानाम् of the Kuru and Pandavas, जुगोप protected, कुक्षिम् within the womb, गतः having entered, आत्त-चक्रः one who holds the discus, मातुः of mother, च also, मे my, यः who, शरणम् refuge, गतायाः having taken

Anvaya: यः आत्तचक्रः, शरणं गतायाः मे मातुः कुक्षिं गतः, कुरुपाण्डवानां सन्तानबीजं, द्रौण्यस्त्रविप्लुष्टम् इदं मत् अङ्गं जुगोप।

Literal meaning of the Anvaya: यः आत्तचक्रः The one who is a wielder of the discus, शरणं गतायाः मे मातुः कुक्षिं गतः entered the womb of my mother who had sought His shelter, कुरुपाण्डवानां सन्तानबीजं the seed of the continuation of the Kuru and Pandava dynasties, द्रौण्यस्त्रविप्लुष्टम् burned by the weapon of Ashwatthama, इदं मत् अङ्गं this, my body, जुगोप protected.

Simple meaning: “When my mother took the shelter of the Lord, who is a wielder of the discus, He entered her womb and protected my body—burned by the weapon of Ashwatthama—which was the seed for the continuation of the Kuru and Pandava dynasties…

वीर्याणि तस्याखिलदेहभाजामन्तर्बहिः पूरुषकालरूपैः ।

प्रयच्छतो मृत्युमुतामृतं च माया मनुष्यस्य वदस्व विद्वन् ॥10.1.7॥

Word-by-word meaning: वीर्याणि the glorious deeds, तस्य of Him, अखिल-देह-भाजाम् of all embodied beings, अन्तः within, बहिः without, पूरुष-काल-रूपैः in the form of the Supreme Person and Time, प्रयच्छतः bestowing, मृत्युम् death, उत also, अमृतम् immortality, च and, मायया by His illusory energy, मनुष्यस्य of appearing as a human, वदस्व please speak, विद्वन् O learned one

Anvaya: विद्वन्! अखिलदेहभाजाम् अन्तः बहिः, पूरुषकालरूपैः मृत्युम् अमृतं च प्रयच्छतः, मायया मनुष्यस्य तस्य वीर्याणि वदस्व।

Literal meaning of the Anvaya: विद्वन्! O learned one, अखिलदेहभाजाम् अन्तः बहिः Of the one who is inside and outside of the embodied beings, पूरुषकालरूपैः of the one who in the form of the Supreme person and Time, मृत्युम् अमृतं च death and immortality, प्रयच्छतः bestows, मायया मनुष्यस्य of the person who has illusory energy, तस्य of him, वीर्याणि वदस्व please tell the glorious deeds.

Simple meaning: “O Learned one! Please describe the glorious deeds of the Lord, who is inside and outside of the embodied beings, who in the form of the Supreme Person and Time bestows death and immortality, who by His illusory energy appears as a human.

रोहिण्यास्तनयः प्रोक्तो रामः सङ्कर्षणस्त्वया ।

देवक्या गर्भसम्बन्धः कुतो देहान्तरं विना ॥10.1.8॥

Word-by-word meaning: रोहिण्याः of Rohini, तनयः son, प्रोक्तः is said, रामः Balarama, सङ्कर्षणः Sankarshana, त्वया by you, देवक्याः of Devaki, गर्भ-सम्बन्धः connection with the womb, कुतः how, देह-अन्तरम् विना without the transference of the body

Anvaya: त्वया रोहिण्याः तनयः रामः सङ्कर्षणः प्रोक्तः। देहान्तरं विना देवक्याः गर्भसम्बन्धः कुतः?

Literal meaning of the Anvaya: त्वया By you, रोहिण्याः तनयः Rohini’s son, रामः सङ्कर्षणः Balarama - Sankarshana प्रोक्तः was said. देहान्तरं विना Without the transfer of the body देवक्याः गर्भसम्बन्धः कुतः how is it possible to be related to Devaki’s womb?

Simple meaning: “You have said that Balarama, known as Sankarshana, was the son of Rohini. But without a transfer of bodies, how could He have had a connection with the womb of Devaki?

कस्मान्मुकुन्दो भगवान् पितुर्गेहाद् व्रजं गतः ।

क्व वासं ज्ञातिभिः सार्धं कृतवान् सात्वतांपतिः ॥10.1.9॥

Word-by-word meaning: कस्मात् why, मुकुन्दः Mukunda, भगवान् the Supreme Lord, पितुः गेहात् from His father’s house, व्रजम् to Vraja, गतः went, क्व where, वासम् residence, ज्ञातिभिः with relatives, सार्धम् together, कृतवान् made, सात्वताम् of the Satvatas, पतिः the Lord

Anvaya: सात्वतां पतिः भगवान् मुकुन्दः पितुः गेहात् व्रजं कस्मात् गतः? ज्ञातिभिः सार्धं क्व वासं कृतवान्?

Literal meaning of the Anvaya: सात्वतां पतिः The Lord of the Satvatas, भगवान् मुकुन्दः the Supreme Lord Mukunda, पितुः गेहात् from his father’s house, व्रजं कस्मात् गतः why did he go to Vraja? ज्ञातिभिः सार्धं Along with his relatives, क्व वासं कृतवान् where did he reside?

Simple meaning: “Why did Lord Mukunda, the Lord of the Satvatas, leave His father’s house and go to Vraja? And where did He reside there along with His relatives?

व्रजे वसन् किमकरोन्मधुपुर्यां च केशवः ।

भ्रातरं चावधीत् कंसं मातुरद्धातदहर्णम् ॥10.1.10॥

Word-by-word meaning: व्रजे in Vraja, वसन् residing, किम् what, अकरोत् did, मधुपुर्याम् in Mathura, च also, केशवः Keshava, भ्रातरम् brother, च also, अवधीत् killed, कंसम् Kamsa, मातुः of His mother, अद्धा clearly, अ-तत् not-(like) that, अर्हणम् deserve

अद्धा   Truly, clearly, surely, undoubtedly, in truth, really, certainly, indeed;  src, kosha ashtadhyayi

Anvaya: केशवः व्रजे मधुपुर्यां च वसन् किम् अकरोत्? च अद्धा अतत् अहर्णं मातुः भ्रातरं कंसम् अवधीत्।

Literal meaning of the Anvaya: केशवः Keshava, व्रजे मधुपुर्यां च वसन् while living in Vraja and Madhupuri (Mathura), किम् अकरोत् what did He do? च अद्धा Also clearly, अतत् अहर्णं not deserving (to be killed on account of being His mother’s brother), मातुः भ्रातरं कंसम् His mother’s brother Kamsa, अवधीत् killed.

Simple meaning: “What did Lord Keshava do while residing in Vraja and Mathura? Also, clearly, He killed His mother’s brother Kamsa who did not deserve to be killed (on account of being His mother’s brother).

देहं मानुषमाश्रित्य कति वर्षाणि वृष्णिभिः ।

यदुपुर्यां सहावात्सीत् पत्न्य: कत्यभवन् प्रभोः॥10.1.11॥

Word-by-word meaning: देहम् body, मानुषम् human, आश्रित्य assuming, कति how many, वर्षाणि years, वृष्णिभिः with the Vrishnis, यदु-पुर्याम् in the city of the Yadus, सह together, अवात्सीत् resided, पत्न्यः wives, कति how many, अभवन् were there, प्रभोः of the Lord

अवात्सीत् - वस् वसँ निवासे भ्वादिः परस्मैपदी अकर्मकः अनिट् (to dwell,to live, to stay) (निवास करना, टिकना) कर्तरि लुङ्लकारः (परस्मैपदम्) प्रथमपुरुषः एकवचनम् (अवात्सीत्)

Anvaya: मानुषं देहम् आश्रित्य, वृष्णिभिः सह यदुपुर्यां कति वर्षाणि अवात्सीत्? प्रभोः कति पत्न्यः अभवन्?

Literal meaning of the Anvaya: मानुषं देहम् आश्रित्य Assuming human body, वृष्णिभिः सह with the Vrishnis, यदुपुर्यां in the city of Yadus, कति वर्षाणि अवात्सीत् how many years, did He reside? प्रभोः कति पत्न्यः अभवन् How many wives were there of the Lord?

Simple meaning: “Assuming a human body, how many years did the Lord reside in the city of the Yadus along with the Vrishnis? And how many wives did the Lord have?

एतदन्यच्च सर्वं मे मुने कृष्णविचेष्टितम् ।

वक्तुमर्हसि सर्वज्ञ श्रद्दधानाय विस्तृतम् ॥10.1.12॥

Word-by-word meaning: एतत् this, अन्यत् other, च and, सर्वम् all, मे to me, मुने O sage, कृष्ण-विचेष्टितम् the activities of Krishna, वक्तुम् to speak, अर्हसि you are fit, सर्वज्ञ O all-knowing one, श्रद्दधानाय to one with faith, विस्तृतम् in detail

Anvaya: मुने! सर्वज्ञ! श्रद्दधानाय मे एतत् अन्यत् च कृष्णविचेष्टितं सर्वं विस्तृतं वक्तुम् अर्हसि।

Literal meaning of the Anvaya: मुने! सर्वज्ञ! O Sage! O All-knowing one!, श्रद्दधानाय मे To me, who has faith, एतत् अन्यत् च this and everything else, कृष्णविचेष्टितं सर्वं all the activities of Krishna, विस्तृतं वक्तुम् अर्हसि please tell me in detail.

Simple meaning: “O Sage! O All-knowing one! Please speak to me in detail, who has faith, about this and all other activities of Krishna.

स्वप्ने यथा पश्यति देहमीदृशं मनोरथेनाभिनिविष्टचेतनः।

दृष्टश्रुताभ्यां मनसानुचिन्तयन् प्रपद्यते तत् किमपि ह्यपस्मृतिः ॥10.1.41॥

Word-by-word meaning: स्वप्ने in a dream, यथा as, पश्यति sees, देहम् body, ईदृशम् like this, मनो-रथेन by imagination, अभिनिविष्ट-चेतनः absorbed in thought, दृष्ट-श्रुताभ्याम् from what has been seen and heard, मनसा with the mind, अनुचिन्तयन् reflecting, प्रपद्यते attains, तत् that (the embodied soul), किमपि another body, हि indeed, अपस्मृतिः forgetfulness (of real self)

Anvaya: यथा स्वप्ने अभिनिविष्टचेतनः मनोरथेन दृष्टश्रुताभ्यां मनसा अनुचिन्तयन् ईदृशं देहं पश्यति, हि तत् अपस्मृतिः किमपि प्रपद्यते।

Literal meaning of the Anvaya: यथा स्वप्ने As in dream-like state, अभिनिविष्टचेतनः the person who is fully absorbed, मनोरथेन in imagination, दृष्टश्रुताभ्यां मनसा अनुचिन्तयन् and reflecting upon things which are seen and heard, ईदृशं देहं पश्यति perceives himself to have the very body (as in dream-state), हि तत् indeed, the embodied soul, अपस्मृतिः (सन्) forgetting the present body, किमपि  other body, प्रपद्यते attains.

Simple meaning: “Just as in a dream a person, absorbed in imagination and reflecting upon things seen and heard, perceives himself to have a body like the one in dream-state (thinks that the dream is real), in the same way, the embodied soul forgetting the present body, attains a new one.

यतो यतो धावति दैवचोदितं मनो विकारात्मकमाप पञ्चसु ।

गुणेषु मायारचितेषु देह्यसौ प्रपद्यमानः सह तेन जायते ॥10.1.42॥

Word-by-word meaning: यतः from wherever, यतः from wherever, धावति runs, दैव-चोदितम् impelled by destiny, मनः mind, विकार-आत्मकम् filled with mental modifications, आपः becoming absorbed, पञ्चसु in the five, गुणेषु in the sense qualities, माय-रचितेषु created by maya, देही the embodied soul, असौ he, प्रपद्यमानः entering into, सह with, तेन that (object), जायते is born

Anvaya: यतः यतः दैवचोदितं विकारात्मकं मनः पञ्चसु मायरचितेषु गुणेषु आपः धावति, असौ देही तेन सह प्रपद्यमानः जायते।

Literal meaning of the Anvaya: यतः यतः Wherever, दैवचोदितं impelled by destiny, विकारात्मकं and mental modifications, मनः the mind, पञ्चसु मायरचितेषु गुणेषु आपः becoming absorbed in the illusion created by the five senses, धावति runs amok, असौ देही that embodied soul, तेन सह along with those mental modifications प्रपद्यमानः जायते takes birth obtaining those qualities.

Simple meaning: “Wherever the mind, driven by destiny and filled with its own agitations, becoming absorbed by the five sense objects created by maya, runs, the embodied soul follows that mind. According to whatever the mind becomes absorbed in, the soul takes birth in alignment with that attachment.

ज्योतिर्यथैव उदकपार्थिवेष्वदः समीरवेगानुगतं विभाव्यते।

एवं स्वमायारचितेष्वसौ पुमान् गुणेषु रागानुगतो विमुह्यति ॥10.1.43॥

Word-by-word meaning: ज्योतिः light, यथा as, एव indeed, उदक-पार्थिवेषु in water and earthly objects, अदः not perceived directly (reflected), समीर-वेग-अनुगतम् carried by the force of wind, विभाव्यते is perceived, एवम् thus, स्व-माया-रचितेषु created by his own maya, असौ this, पुमान् embodied soul, गुणेषु in the qualities, राग-अनुगतः following attachment, विमुह्यति becomes deluded

अदः परोक्षभूतं src. Kosha Ashtadyayi

Anvaya: यथा उदकपार्थिवेषु ज्योतिः अदः समीरवेगानुगतं विभाव्यते, एवम् असौ पुमान् स्वमायारचितेषु गुणेषु रागानुगतः विमुह्यति।

Literal meaning of the Anvaya: यथा just as, उदकपार्थिवेषु in water or earthly objects, ज्योतिः light, अदः reflected, समीरवेगानुगतम् moving according to the motion of the wind, विभाव्यते is seen— एवम् in the same way, असौ पुमान् this embodied soul, स्वमायारचितेषु in objects created by his own maya, गुणेषु in the sense qualities, रागानुगतः following attachment, विमुह्यति becomes confused or deluded.

Simple meaning: “Just as the reflection of light in water or on earthly surfaces appears to move when the wind moves, similarly the embodied soul, seeing the objects created by his own maya and following his attachments to the sense qualities, becomes deluded.

My notes: The Sun is a steady source of light. It does not move. But its reflection in the water moves due to the movement caused by the wind. So it seems that the Sun itself is moving. Similarly, a person influenced by the forces of Maya, feels this world as real and is under delusion. The effect of wind on the reflection of the Sun on the water is compared to the effect of Maya on the person which makes him assume the world as real.

तस्मान्न कस्यचित् द्रोहमाचरेत् स तथाविधः ।

आत्मनः क्षेममन्विच्छन् द्रोग्धुर्वै परतो भयम् ॥10.1.44॥

Word-by-word meaning: तस्मात् therefore, न not, कस्यचित् to anyone, द्रोहम् harm, आचरेत् should do, सः he, तथा-विधः such a person, आत्मनः for oneself, क्षेमम् welfare, अन्विच्छन् desiring, द्रोग्धुः of one who bears ill-will, वै indeed, परतः from others, भयम् fear

Anvaya: तस्मात् तथाविधः सः आत्मनः क्षेमम् अन्विच्छन् कस्यचित् द्रोहं न आचरेत्, द्रोग्धुः परतः भयं वै (प्राप्नोति)।

Literal meaning of the Anvaya:  तस्मात् Therefore, तथाविधः सः such a type of person, आत्मनः क्षेमम् अन्विच्छन् desiring his own well-being, कस्यचित् द्रोहं न आचरेत् never does evil to anyone. द्रोग्धुः One who harbours ill-will, परतः from others, भयं वै (प्राप्नोति) will become fearful.

Simple meaning: “A person who wants his own well-being should never harm anyone. Because when someone harms others, fear and danger come back to him from others who will seek retribution in future.

एषा तवानुजा बाला कृपणा पुत्रिकोपमा ।

हन्तुं नार्हसि कल्याणीमिमां त्वं दीनवत्सलः ॥10.1.45॥

Word-by-word meaning: एषा this girl, तव your, अनुजा younger sister, बाला child, कृपणा helpless, पुत्रिका-उपमा like a daughter, हन्तुम् to kill, न not, अर्हसि you should, कल्याणीम् auspicious one, इमाम् this, त्वम् you, दीन-वत्सलः protector of the distressed

Anvaya: एषा कृपणा बाला तव अनुजा पुत्रिकोपमा (अस्ति)। दीनवत्सलः त्वम् इमां कल्याणीं हन्तुं न अर्हसि।

Literal meaning of the Anvaya: एषा कृपणा बाला This helpless girl is, तव अनुजा your younger sister, पुत्रिकोपमा and like your daughter. दीनवत्सलः त्वम् You who are kind hearted, इमां कल्याणीं  this auspicious one, हन्तुं न अर्हसि ought not to kill.

Simple meaning: “This helpless girl is your younger sister and like a daughter to you. You who are kind hearted ought not to kill this auspicious one.”

श्री शुक उवाच

निर्बन्धं तस्य तं ज्ञात्वा विचिन्त्यानकदुन्दुभिः।

प्राप्तं कालं प्रतिव्योढुमिदं तत्रान्वपद्यत ॥10.1.47॥

Word-by-word meaning: निर्बन्धम् firm resolve, तस्य his, तम् that, ज्ञात्वा knowing, विचिन्त्य having considered, आनकदुन्दुभिः Vasudeva, प्राप्तम् come, कालम् time, प्रतिव्योढुम् to counter, इदम् this, तत्र there, अन्वपद्यत undertook, followed

Anvaya: आनकदुन्दुभिः तस्य तं निर्बन्धं ज्ञात्वा, विचिन्त्य, प्राप्तं कालं प्रतिव्योढुं तत्र इदम् अन्वपद्यत।

Literal meaning of the Anvaya: आनकदुन्दुभिः Anakdundubhi, तस्य तं निर्बन्धं ज्ञात्वा knowing the firm resolve of Kamsa, विचिन्त्य thinking, प्राप्तं कालं प्रतिव्योढुं about how to counter the situation, तत्र इदम् अन्वपद्यत then decided on this.

Simple meaning: Vasudeva, knowing that his (Kamsa’s) resolve was firm, decided upon this course of action to alleviate the situation.

मृत्युर्बुद्धिमतापोह्यो यावद् बुद्धिबलोदयम् ।

यद्यसौ न निवर्तेत नापराधोऽस्ति देहिनः ॥10.1.48॥

Word-by-word meaning: मृत्युः death, बुद्धिमता by an intelligent person, अपोह्यः to be avoided, यावत् until, बुद्धि-बल-उदयम् rise of strength of intelligence, यदि if, असौ he, न not, निवर्तेत turns back, न not, अपराधः fault, अस्ति is, देहिनः of the embodied person

Anvaya: बुद्धिमता मृत्युः यावत् बुद्धिबलोदयम् (तावत्) अपोह्यः। यदि असौ (मृत्युः) न निवर्तेत, देहिनः अपराधः न अस्ति।

Literal meaning of the Anvaya: बुद्धिमता By the wise person, मृत्युः death, यावत् बुद्धिबलोदयम् until there is intelligence, (तावत्) अपोह्यः till then, should be prevented. यदि असौ (मृत्युः) If the death, न निवर्तेत is not averted, देहिनः of the embodied beings, अपराधः न अस्ति there is no fault.

Simple meaning: A wise person should try to save himself from the danger ( “avoid death” means saving oneself from danger in this case) through intelligence and strength until he finds a way. But if death (danger) cannot be avoided, the person is not at fault—because every embodied being is subject to destiny.

प्रदाय मृत्यवे पुत्रान् मोचये कृपणामिमाम् ।

सुता मे यदि जायेरन् मृत्युर्वा न म्रियेत चेत् ॥10.1.49॥

Word-by-word meaning: प्रदाय giving over, मृत्यवे to death (personified Kamsa), पुत्रान् the sons, मोचये I shall release, कृपणाम् the helpless one, इमाम् this (girl / Devaki), सुताः sons, मे my, यदि if, जायेरन् maybe born, मृत्युः Death (personified Kamsa), वा or, न not, म्रियेत might die, चेत् if

Anvaya: (अहं) पुत्रान् मृत्यवे प्रदाय इमां कृपणां मोचये। यदि मे सुताः जायेरन्। न चेत्, मृत्युः म्रियेत वा।

Literal meaning of the Anvaya: (अहं) I, पुत्रान् मृत्यवे प्रदाय giving my sons to death (Kamsa is being referred to as death personified), इमां कृपणां मोचये will save this helpless one (Devaki). यदि मे सुताः जायेरन्  My sons may be born. न चेत् Or else, मृत्युः म्रियेत वा death (personified Kamsa) himself may die.

Simple meaning: By giving my sons to death (to Kamsa), I will save this helpless Devaki. My sons may be born. Or else, Kamsa himself may die.

विपर्ययो वा किं न स्याद् गतिर्धातुर्दुरत्यया ।

Word-by-word meaning: विपर्ययः contrary, वा or, किम् what, न not, स्यात् may be, गतिः ways, धातुः of the Creator, दुरत्यया unfathomable, उपस्थितः what is imminent, निवर्तेत may go back, निवृत्तः which has been averted, पुनः again, आपतेत् may come back

अग्नेर्यथा दारुवियोगयोगयोरदृष्टोऽन्यन्न निमित्तमस्ति ।

Word-by-word meaning: अग्नेः of fire, यथा just as, दारु-वियोग-योगयोः in separation and combination with wood, अदृष्टः unseen, अन्यत् other, निमित्तम् cause, अस्ति there is, एवम् thus, हि indeed, जन्तोः of a living being, अपि also, दुर्विभाव्यः difficult to understand, शरीर-संयोग-वियोग-हेतुः the cause of the body’s union and separation

Anvaya: यथा अग्नेः दारुवियोगयोगयोः अन्यत् निमित्तम् अदृष्टः अस्ति, एवं हि जन्तोः अपि शरीरसंयोगवियोगहेतुः दुर्विभाव्यः।

Literal meaning of the Anvaya: यथा अग्नेः दारुवियोगयोगयोः Just as the fire coming in contact with wood (burning it) or leaving it (without burning), अन्यत् निमित्तम् अदृष्टः अस्ति no other cause is seen; एवं हि similarly, जन्तोः अपि of a living being too, शरीरसंयोगवियोगहेतुः दुर्विभाव्यः the cause of the body’s union (with other beings) and separation (from them) is difficult to comprehend.

Simple meaning: Just as one cannot determine which tree a forest fire will touch and which it will leave untouched—there being no cause other than the unseen destiny—so too, for embodied beings, it is difficult to know which body will come in contact with which, and from which it will separate. Only destiny governs these unions and separations.

एवं विमृश्य तं पापं यावदात्मनिदर्शनम् ।

Word-by-word meaning: वसुदेवः उवाच Vasudeva said, न not, हि indeed, अस्याः of her (the bodiless being), ते to you, भयम् fear, सौम्य O gentle one, यत् because, वाक् speech, आह said, अशरीरिणी a bodiless being, पुत्रान् sons, समर्पयिष्ये I will hand over, अस्याः of her (Devaki’s), यतः from whom, ते your, भयम् fear, उत्थितम् has arisen

Anvaya: वसुदेवः उवाच — सौम्य! यत् वाक् अशरीरिणी आह, अस्याः ते भयं न हि। यतः ते भयम् उत्थितम्, अस्याः पुत्रान् समर्पयिष्ये।

Literal meaning of the Anvaya: वसुदेवः उवाच Vasudeva said — सौम्य! O Gentle one!, यत् वाक् whatever words, अशरीरिणी आह the bodiless being said, अस्याः from her (Devaki), ते to you, भयं न हि there is no fear. यतः Because of whom, ते to you, भयम् उत्थितम् fear has arisen, अस्याः पुत्रान् her children (Devaki’s), समर्पयिष्ये I will offer.

Simple meaning: Vasudeva said: “O gentle one! There is no cause of fear from Devaki because of whatever the bodiless being has uttered. I will offer her (Devaki’s) sons to you because of whom the fear which has arisen.”

स्वसुर्वधान्निववृते कंसस्तद्वाक्यसारवित् ।

Simple meaning: As the time passed, year after year, Devaki who was endowed with divine qualities gave birth to eight sons and a daughter (Subhadra).

Anvaya: आनकदुन्दुभिः सः अनृतात् अतिविह्वलः, प्रथमजं कीर्तिमन्तं कंसाय, कृच्छ्रेण अर्पयामास।

Literal meaning of the Anvaya: आनकदुन्दुभिः सः He, Anakdundubhi, अनृतात् from falsehood, अतिविह्वलः very much afraid of going against (his promise), प्रथमजं कीर्तिमन्तं his first-born named Kirtiman, कंसाय to Kamsa, कृच्छ्रेण with great difficulty, अर्पयामास offered.

Simple meaning: Vasudeva, who was very conscious of not breaking his promise, handed over his first-born son named Kirtiman to Kamsa with great difficulty.

किं दु:सहं नु साधूनां विदुषां किमपेक्षितम् ।

किमकार्यं कदर्याणां दुस्त्यजं किं धृतात्मनाम् ॥10.1.58॥

Word-by-word meaning: किम् what, दुःसहम् unbearable, नु indeed, साधूनाम् of the noble, विदुषाम् of the wise, किम् what, अपेक्षितम् desired, किम् what, अ-कार्यम् should not be done, कदर्याणाम् of the miserly/mean, दुस्त्यजम् hard to give up, किम् what, धृतात्मनाम् of the self-controlled

Anvaya: साधूनां किं दु:सहं नु? विदुषां किम् अपेक्षितम्? कदर्याणां किम् अकार्यम्? धृतात्मनां किं दुस्त्यजम्?

Literal meaning of the Anvaya: साधूनां For the noble, किं दु:सहं नु what is truly unbearable? विदुषां For the wise, किम् अपेक्षितम् what is left to be desired? कदर्याणां For the miserly, किम् अकार्यम् what is considered improper? धृतात्मनां For the self-controlled, किं दुस्त्यजम् what is difficult to give up?

Simple meaning: What can ever be unbearable for noble people? What would the wise ever crave for? What action would a miser ever hesitate to do? And what is truly difficult to renounce for those who have full control over themselves?

दृष्ट्वा समत्वं तच्छौरेः सत्ये चैव व्यवस्थितिम् ।

कंसस्तुष्टमना राजन् हसन्निदमब्रवीत् ॥10.1.59॥

Word-by-word meaning: दृष्ट्वा having seen, समत्वम् composure, तत् that, शौरेः of Shauri (Son of Shura - Vasudeva), सत्ये in truthfulness, च and, एव indeed, व्यवस्थितिम् firmness, कंसः Kamsa, तुष्टमना pleased in mind, राजन् O King (Parikshita), हसन् smiling, इदम् this, अब्रवीत् said

Literal meaning of the Anvaya: राजन्! O King! कंसः Kamsa, तत् शौरेः that son of Shura’s, समत्वं composure, सत्ये च एव व्यवस्थितिं and resolution of being situated in truth, दृष्ट्वा seeing, तुष्टमनाः pleased in mind, हसन् इदम् अब्रवीत् smiling said this.

प्रतियातु कुमारोऽयं न ह्यस्मादस्ति मे भयम् ।

अष्टमाद् युवयोर्गर्भान्मृत्युर्मे विहितः किल ॥10.1.60॥

Word-by-word meaning: प्रतियातु let him go back, कुमारः boy, अयम् this, न not, हि indeed, अस्मात् from this (child), अस्ति is, मे to me, भयम् fear, अष्टमात् from the eighth, युवयोः of you two, गर्भात् from the womb, मृत्युः death, मे for me, विहितः destined, किल indeed

Literal meaning of the Anvaya: अयं कुमारः प्रतियातु Take this child back. अस्मात् From him, मे to me, भयं न हि अस्ति there is no fear; युवयोः Of both of you, अष्टमात् गर्भात् from eighth child, मे मृत्युः my death, विहितः किल is destined indeed.

तथेति सुतमादाय ययावानकदुन्दुभिः।

Word-by-word meaning: तथा इति “so be it” (saying) thus, सुतम् son, आदाय taking, ययौ went, आनकदुन्दुभिः Anakadundubhi (Vasudeva), न not, अभ्यनन्दत approved, तत् that, वाक्यम् speech, असतः of the untruthful, अ-विजित-आत्मनः of the one who has not conquered his mind

Literal meaning of the Anvaya: आनाकदुन्दुभिः Anakdundubhi, तथा इति saying “so be it” सुतम् आदाय taking his son, ययौ went away. असतः Of the untruthful, अविजितात्मनः of the one who had not conquered his mind, तत् वाक्यं those words, न अभ्यनन्दत did not trust.

Simple meaning: Vasudeva, saying “So be it,” took the child and left. But he did not approve (trust) the words of the evil (Kamsa).

Anvaya: व्रजे नन्दाद्याः ये गोपाः, अमीषां याः योषितः च, वसुदेवाद्याः वृष्णयः, देवक्याद्याः यदुस्त्रियः —

Simple meaning: Nanda, the cowherds of Vraja, and their women, as well as Vasudeva, the other Vrishnis, Devaki and other Yadu women —

सर्वे वै देवताप्राया उभयोरपि भारत।

ज्ञातयो बन्धुसुहृदो ये च कंसमनुव्रताः ॥10.1.63॥

Word-by-word meaning: सर्वे all, वै indeed, देवता-प्रायाः almost like gods, उभयोः of both (groups), अपि also, भारत O descendant of Bharata (Parikshita) ज्ञातयः relatives, बन्धु-सुहृतः kinsmen and well-wishers, ये who, च also, कंसम् अनुव्रताः followers of Kamsa

Anvaya: भारत! ज्ञातयः बन्धुसुहृतः, कंसं ये अनुव्रताः च – उभयोः सर्वे अपि देवताप्रायाः।

Word-by-word meaning: एतत् this, कंसाय to Kamsa, भगवान् the divine sage (Narada), शशंस narrated, अभ्येत्य having arrived, नारदः Narada, भूमेः of the earth, भारायमाणानाम् of those who were increasing the burden, दैत्यानाम् of the daityas, च and, वध-उद्यमम् the effort for their destruction

Simple meaning: Narada came to Kamsa and informed him of this—namely, the plan of the gods to destroy the daityas who were increasing the burden of the earth.

आत्मानमिह सञ्जातं जानन् प्राग् विष्णुना हतम् ।

महासुरं कालनेमिं यदुभिः स व्यरुध्यत ॥10.1.68॥

Word-by-word meaning: आत्मानम् himself, इह here (in this life), सञ्जातम् born, जानन् knowing, प्राक् formerly, विष्णुना by Vishnu, हतम् slain, महा-असुरम् great asura, कालनेमिम् Kalanemi, यदुभिः with the Yadus, सः he (Kamsa), व्यरुध्यत became hostile / fought

Anvaya: प्राक् विष्णुना हतं महासुरं कालनेमिं सः आत्मानम् इह सञ्जातं जानन्, यदुभिः व्यरुध्यत।

Literal meaning of the Anvaya: प्राक् Earlier, विष्णुना हतं killed by Vishnu, महासुरं कालनेमिं the great asura Kalanemi, सः him (Kalanemi), आत्मानम् to be himself (Kamsa), इह सञ्जातं born now, जानन् knowing, यदुभिः व्यरुध्यत was hostile towards the Yadus.

Simple meaning: Knowing himself to be the great asura Kalanemi who had been slain by Vishnu in his previous birth, and knowing himself to be re-born here (in this life), Kamsa became hostile toward the Yadus.

उग्रसेनं च पितरं यदुभोजान्धकाधिपम् ।

स्वयं निगृह्य बुभुजे शूरसेनान् महाबलः ॥10.1.69॥

Word-by-word meaning: उग्रसेनम् Ugrasena, च and, पितरम् father, यदु-भोज-अन्धक-अधिपम् the ruler of the Yadus, Bhojas, and Andhakas, स्वयम् himself, निगृह्य having imprisoned, बुभुजे enjoyed / seized, शूरसेनान् the kingdom of the Shurasenas, महा-बलः the very powerful one (Kamsa)

Anvaya: महाबलः कंसः यदुभोजान्धकाधिपं पितरम् उग्रसेनं निगृह्य, स्वयं शूरसेनान् बुभुजे।

Literal meaning of the Anvaya: महाबलः कंसः The mighty Kamsa, यदुभोजान्धकाधिपं the ruler of the Yadus, Bhojas, and Andhakas, पितरम् उग्रसेनं his father Ugrasena, निगृह्य having imprisoned, स्वयं शूरसेनान् बुभुजे ruled over the kingdom of Shurasena himself.

Simple meaning: The powerful Kamsa imprisoned his father Ugrasena, who was the ruler of the Yadus, Bhojas, and Andhakas, and then seized and ruled the kingdom of the Shurasenas himself.
