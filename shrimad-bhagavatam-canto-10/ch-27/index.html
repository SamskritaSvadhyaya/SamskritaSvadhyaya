Chapter 27

Indra extolls Shri Krishna

श्रीमद्भागवतमहापुराणम्

दशमः स्कन्धः

इन्द्रस्तुतिर्नाम नाम सप्तविंशः अध्यायः

(28 Shlokas)

श्री शुक उवाच

गोवर्धने धृते शैल आसाराद् रक्षिते व्रजे ।

गोलोकादाव्रजत् कृष्णं सुरभिः शक्र एव च ॥10.27.1॥

Word-by-word meaning: श्री शुकः उवाच Shri Shuka said, गोवर्धने Govardhan, धृते being held, शैले the mountain, आसारात् from the heavy downpour, रक्षिते being protected, व्रजे the land of Vraja, गो-लोकात् from the world of the cows in heaven (Goloka), आव्रजत् came, कृष्णम् toward Krishna, सुरभिः Mother Surabhi (the celestial cow), शक्रः Indra, एव indeed, च and

Anvaya: श्री शुकः उवाच – गोवर्धने शैले धृते (सति), आसारात् व्रजे रक्षिते (सति), सुरभिः शक्रः च एव गोलोकात् कृष्णम् आव्रजत्।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: श्री शुकः उवाच Shri Shuka said, गोवर्धने शैले धृते (सति) When the Govardhan mountain had been held up, आसारात् व्रजे रक्षिते (सति) and the land of Vraja had been protected from the torrential rain, सुरभिः शक्रः च एव both Mother Surabhi and Indra, गोलोकात् कृष्णम् आव्रजत् came from the celestial cow-planet toward Krishna.

Simple Meaning: Shri Shuka said: After Krishna had held up Govardhan Hill and successfully shielded the residents of Vraja from the devastating storms, Indra and the celestial cow Surabhi descended from the spiritual realm of Goloka to visit Him. Indra, now humbled and aware of Krishna's divinity, came to offer his apologies and worship.

विविक्त उपसङ्गम्य व्रीडितः कृतहेलनः ।

पस्पर्श पादयोरेनं केरीटेनार्कवर्चसा ॥10.27.2॥

Word-by-word meaning: विविक्ते in a secluded place, उपसङ्गम्य approaching, व्रीडितः feeling ashamed, कृत-हेलनः having committed an offense (disrespect), पस्पर्श he touched, पादयोः the two feet, एनम् Him (Krishna), किरीटेन with his crown, अर्क-वर्चसा which was as brilliant as the sun

Anvaya: (सः शक्रः) कृतहेलनः व्रीडितः (सन्) विविक्ते उपसङ्गम्य अर्कवर्चसा किरीटेन एनं पादयोः पस्पर्श।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: (सः शक्रः) कृतहेलनः व्रीडितः (सन्) That Indra, having committed an offense and feeling deeply ashamed, विविक्ते उपसङ्गम्य approached Krishna in a secluded place, अर्कवर्चसा किरीटेन and with his crown which was as brilliant as the sun, एनं पादयोः पस्पर्श touched His (Krishna's) two feet.

Simple Meaning: Deeply ashamed of the great offense he had committed by attacking Vraja, Indra approached Shri Krishna in a private place. He prostrated himself before the Lord, and as he bowed his head, his magnificent crown—which shone with the brilliance of the sun—touched the lotus feet of Krishna in a gesture of complete surrender and repentance.

दृष्टश्रुतानुभावोऽस्य कृष्णस्यामिततेजसः ।

नष्टत्रिलोकेशमद इन्द्र आह कृताञ्जलिः ॥10.27.3॥

Word-by-word meaning: दृष्ट-श्रुत-अनुभावः having seen and heard of the power, अस्य of this, कृष्णस्य of Krishna, अमित-तेजसः of unlimited potency, नष्ट-त्रिलोक-ईश-मदः whose pride as the lord of the three worlds was destroyed, इन्द्रः Indra, आह said, कृत-अञ्जलिः with joined palms

Anvaya: अस्य अमिततेजसः कृष्णस्य दृष्टश्रुतानुभावः नष्टत्रिलोकेशमदः इन्द्रः कृताञ्जलिः (सन्) आह।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: अस्य अमिततेजसः कृष्णस्य Of this Krishna of unlimited potency, दृष्टश्रुतानुभावः having now seen and heard the greatness, नष्टत्रिलोकेशमदः his pride as the master of the three worlds having been shattered, इन्द्रः कृताञ्जलिः (सन्) आह Indra, with palms joined in prayer, spoke.

Simple Meaning: Indra had now personally witnessed and heard of the inconceivable power of Shri Krishna. His intoxicating pride, rooted in his position as the ruler of the three worlds, was completely washed away. Standing before the Lord with folded hands in a posture of deep respect, Indra began to offer his prayers.

इन्द्र उवाच​

विशुद्धसत्त्वं तव धाम शान्तं तपोमयं ध्वस्तरजस्तमस्कम् ।

मायामयोऽयं गुणसम्प्रवाहो न विद्यते तेऽग्रहणानुबन्धः ॥10.27.4॥

Word-by-word meaning: इन्द्र उवाच​ Indra said, विशुद्ध-सत्त्वम् pure goodness (unmixed with material qualities), तव Your, धाम form/abode/nature, शान्तम् undisturbed/peaceful, तपो-मयम् full of knowledge and spiritual power, ध्वस्त-रजः-तमस्कम् where the qualities of passion and ignorance are destroyed (absent), माया-मयः consisting of Maya (illusion), अयम् this, गुण-सम्प्रवाहः the continuous flow of the material modes, न विद्यते does not exist, ते in You, अग्रहण-अनुबन्धः which is based on the bond of ignorance (or the tendency to accept a material body)

Anvaya: इन्द्र उवाच​ – तव धाम विशुद्धसत्त्वं शान्तं तपोमयं ध्वस्तरजस्तमस्कम् (अस्ति)। मायामयः अयं गुणसम्प्रवाहः ते न विद्यते, (यतः अयम्) अग्रहणानुबन्धः (अस्ति)।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: इन्द्र उवाच​ Indra said, तव धाम विशुद्धसत्त्वं शान्तं तपोमयं ध्वस्तरजस्तमस्कम् (अस्ति) Your form and state of being are situated in pure goodness, peaceful, full of spiritual knowledge, and entirely free from the influence of passion and ignorance. मायामयः अयं गुणसम्प्रवाहः ते न विद्यते This material flow of the modes of nature, which is a product of Maya, does not exist in You, (यतः अयम्) अग्रहणानुबन्धः (अस्ति) because this flow is based on the darkness of ignorance (which cannot touch You).

Simple Meaning: Indra prays: "My Lord, Your transcendental form is established in 'Vishuddha-Sattva'—pure goodness that is beyond the material world. You are eternally peaceful, full of divine knowledge, and untouched by the qualities of passion or ignorance. The turbulent flow of the material modes, created by Your illusory energy (Maya), has no place in You. Such material bondage is born of ignorance, and since You are the source of all light and knowledge, it can never affect Your divine nature."

कुतो नु तद्धेतव ईश तत्कृता लोभादयो येऽबुधलिङ्गभावाः ।

तथापि दण्डं भगवान् बिभर्ति धर्मस्य गुप्त्यै खलनिग्रहाय ॥10.27.5॥

Word-by-word meaning: कुतः from where, नु indeed, तत्-हेतवः the causes of those (material bodies), ईश O Supreme Lord, तत्-कृताः created by those (causes), लोभ-आदयः greed and so on, ये which, अ-बुध-लिङ्ग-भावाः symptoms of an ignorant person, तथापि nevertheless, दण्डम् punishment/scepter, भगवान् the Supreme Lord, बिभर्ति carries/wields, धर्मस्य of religious principles, गुप्त्यै for the protection, खल-निग्रहाय for the subduing of the wicked

Anvaya: ईश! तद्धेतवः (रजस्तमःप्रभृतयः) कुतः नु? तत्कृताः लोभादयः ये अबुधलिङ्गभावाः (ते कुतः)? तथापि भगवान् धर्मस्य गुप्त्यै खलनिग्रहाय दण्डं बिभर्ति।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: ईश! तद्धेतवः (रजस्तमःप्रभृतयः) कुतः नु? O Lord, how can the causes of material bondage (such as passion and ignorance) exist in You? तत्कृताः लोभादयः ये अबुधलिङ्गभावाः (ते कुतः)? Consequently, how could greed and other such emotions, which are the symptoms of an ignorant person, exist in You? तथापि भगवान् धर्मस्य गुप्त्यै खलनिग्रहाय दण्डं बिभर्ति Nevertheless, for the protection of righteousness and the subduing of the wicked, Your Lordship wields the rod of punishment.

Simple Meaning: Indra says: "O Supreme Lord, since You are beyond the material world, the causes of material bondage—the modes of passion and ignorance—cannot exist in You. Therefore, greed, anger, and other such emotions, which are signs of an ignorant soul, have no place in Your nature. Yet, when You punish the wicked (as You have just punished my pride), You do so without any material malice. You wield the rod of punishment only to protect the principles of Dharma and to bring the rebellious to the right path."

पिता गुरुस्त्वं जगतामधीशो दुरत्ययः काल उपात्तदण्डः ।

हिताय स्वेच्छातनुभिः समीहसे मानं विधुन्वञ्जगदीशमानिनाम् ॥10.27.6॥

Word-by-word meaning: पिता the father, गुरुः the spiritual master, त्वम् You, जगताम् of the universes, अधीशः the supreme controller, दुरत्ययः insurmountable, कालः time, उपात्त-दण्डः who wields the rod of punishment, हिताय for the benefit, स्वेच्छा-तनुभिः by Your forms assumed by Your own will, समीहसे You act/endeavor, मानम् the false pride, विधुन्वन् shaking off/destroying, जगत्-ईश-मानिनाम् of those who think themselves to be the lords of the universe

Anvaya: त्वं जगतां पिता गुरुः अधीशः (च असि)। (त्वम्) उपात्तदण्डः दुरत्ययः कालः (असि)। जगदीशमानिनां मानं विधुन्वन् स्वेच्छातनुभिः हिताय समीहसे।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: त्वं जगतां पिता गुरुः अधीशः (च असि) You are the father, the teacher, and the supreme controller of all the universes. (त्वम्) उपात्तदण्डः दुरत्ययः कालः (असि) You are insurmountable time, who wields the rod of punishment. जगदीशमानिनां मानं विधुन्वन् Destroying the false pride of those who imagine themselves to be the lords of the universe, स्वेच्छातनुभिः हिताय समीहसे You perform activities through Your various forms, assumed by Your own will, for the ultimate benefit of all.

Simple Meaning: Indra continues: "My Lord, You are the supreme father, the original spiritual master, and the ultimate ruler of all creation. You are an insurmountable time, the power that eventually punishes all who stray. When You descend in Your various forms—which You assume by Your own divine will—You do so for the welfare of the world. By subduing the arrogance of those who, like myself, foolishly imagine themselves to be the masters of the universe, You lead us back to the path of truth."

ये मद्विधाज्ञा जगदीशमानिनस्त्वां वीक्ष्य कालेऽभयमाशु तन्मदम् ।

हित्वाऽऽर्यमार्गं प्रभजन्त्यपस्मया ईहा खलानामपि तेऽनुशासनम् ॥10.27.7॥

Word-by-word meaning: ये those who, मत्-विधा-अज्ञाः ignorant fools like me, जगत्-ईश-मानिनः thinking ourselves to be the lords of the universe, त्वाम् You, वीक्ष्य seeing, काले in the form of Time, अभयम् fearless, आशु quickly, तत्-मदम् that pride, हित्वा giving up, आर्य-मार्गम् the path of the noble (the path of devotion), प्रभजन्ति they take to, अपस्मयाः free from pride, ईहा the activities (punishment), खलानाम् of the wicked, अपि even, ते Your, अनुशासनम् mercy/instruction

Anvaya: ये मद्विधाज्ञाः जगदीशमानिनः (सन्ति, ते) काले अभयं त्वां वीक्ष्य आशु तन्मदं हित्वा अपस्मयाः (सन्तः) आर्यमार्गं प्रभजन्ति। खलानाम् ईहा अपि ते अनुशासनम् (अस्ति)।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: ये मद्विधाज्ञाः जगदीशमानिनः (सन्ति, ते) Those ignorant persons like me who consider themselves the lords of the universe, काले अभयं त्वां वीक्ष्य upon seeing You as fearless Time, आशु तन्मदं हित्वा quickly abandon that pride, अपस्मयाः (सन्तः) आर्यमार्गं प्रभजन्ति and, becoming humble, take to the path of the noble. खलानाम् ईहा अपि ते अनुशासनम् (अस्ति) Even Your actions toward the wicked are actually Your way of providing instruction and discipline.

Simple Meaning: Indra admits: "Fools like me, intoxicated by power, falsely imagine we are the masters of this world. But when we encounter You—the fearless and eternal Time—our pride quickly vanishes. By subduing our ego, You lead us back to the path of the righteous. Even when You seem to act harshly toward the wicked, it is truly Your mercy in disguise, intended to teach us and correct our path."

स त्वं ममैश्वर्यमदप्लुतस्य कृतागसस्तेऽविदुषः प्रभावम् ।

क्षन्तुं प्रभोऽथार्हसि मूढचेतसो मैवं पुनर्भून्मतिरीश मेऽसती ॥10.27.8॥

Word-by-word meaning: सः he/that (person), त्वम् You, मम my, ऐश्वर्य-मद-प्लुतस्य of one flooded with the intoxication of power/opulence, कृत-आगसः of one who has committed an offense, ते Your, अविदुषः not knowing, प्रभावम् influence/power, क्षन्तुम् to forgive, प्रभो O Lord, अथ therefore, अर्हसि please/You ought, मूढ-चेतसः of one whose mind is bewildered, मा let not, एवम् thus, पुनः again, भूत् let there be, मतिः intelligence/mentality, ईश O Supreme Controller, मे my, असती wicked/impure

Anvaya: प्रभो! ऐश्वर्यमदप्लुतस्य ते प्रभावम् अविदुषः कृतागसः मूढचेतसः मम (अपराधं) सः त्वं क्षन्तुम् अर्हसि। ईश! मे पुनः एवम् असती मतिः मा भूत्।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: प्रभो! ऐश्वर्यमदप्लुतस्य O Lord! Of one flooded by the intoxication of power, ते प्रभावम् अविदुषः of one who did not know Your true power, कृतागसः मूढचेतसः मम (अपराधं) of me, who has committed an offense with a bewildered mind, सः त्वं क्षन्तुम् अर्हसि You should please forgive (that offense). ईश! मे पुनः एवम् असती मतिः मा भूत् O Supreme Controller! Let such a wicked intelligence never arise in me again.

Simple Meaning: Indra pleads: "O Lord, I was blinded and intoxicated by my own power and opulence. Being ignorant of Your true supreme influence, I committed a grave offense with a bewildered mind. I beg You, my Lord, to please forgive me. And O Controller of all, I pray that such foolish and wicked pride never takes root in my heart ever again."

तवावतारोऽयमधोक्षजेह स्वयम्भराणामुरुभारजन्मनाम् ।

चमूपतीनामभवाय देव भवाय युष्मच्चरणानुवर्तिनाम् ॥10.27.9॥

Word-by-word meaning: तव Your, अवतारः incarnation, अयम् this, अधोक्षज O Transcendental Lord (beyond material senses), इह in this world, स्वयम्-भराणाम् of those who are a burden to themselves (or the earth), उरु-भार-जन्मनाम् who have appeared as a great burden, चमू-पतीनाम् of the leaders of armies, अ-भवाय for the destruction, देव O Lord, भवाय for the well-being/prosperity, युष्मत्-चरण-अनुवर्तिनाम् of those who follow Your lotus feet

Anvaya: अधोक्षज! देव! इह तव अयम् अवतारः उरुभारजन्मनां स्वयम्भराणां चमूपतीनाम् अभवाय, युष्मच्चरणानुवर्तिनां (च) भवाय (अस्ति)।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: अधोक्षज! देव! O Transcendental One! O Lord!, इह तव अयम् अवतारः This incarnation of Yours in this world, उरुभारजन्मनां स्वयम्भराणां चमूपतीनाम् अभवाय is for the destruction of the various army commanders who have become a great burden to the earth, युष्मच्चरणानुवर्तिनां (च) भवाय (अस्ति) and for the spiritual prosperity and protection of those who follow Your lotus feet.

Simple Meaning: Indra continues: "O Transcendental Lord, Your descent into this world serves a divine purpose. You have appeared to remove the heavy burden of arrogant military leaders and kings who, being full of themselves, oppress the earth. At the same time, Your appearance is for the highest well-being and joy of Your devotees—those who humble themselves and follow the path of Your lotus feet."

नमस्तुभ्यं भगवते पुरुषाय महात्मने ।

वासुदेवाय कृष्णाय सात्वतां पतये नमः॥10.27.10॥

Word-by-word meaning: नमः obeisances, तुभ्यम् unto You, भगवते the Supreme Personality of Godhead (full of all opulences), पुरुषाय the Supreme Person/Original Being, महात्मने the Great Soul/the Supersoul, वासुदेवाय the son of Vasudeva (the all-pervading Lord), कृष्णाय unto Krishna, सात्वताम् of the devotees (the Yadus/pure-hearted ones), पतये the Lord/Master, नमः obeisances

Anvaya: भगवते महात्मने पुरुषाय तुभ्यं नमः। वासुदेवाय कृष्णाय सात्वतां पतये नमः।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: भगवते महात्मने पुरुषाय तुभ्यं नमः Obeisances unto You, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, the Great Soul, and the Original Person. वासुदेवाय कृष्णाय सात्वतां पतये नमः Obeisances unto Vasudeva, unto Krishna, and unto the Lord and Master of all the devotees.

Simple Meaning: Indra bows down in total surrender, saying: "I offer my humble obeisances unto You, the Supreme Personality of Godhead, the Great Soul who pervades everything. I bow to You, the Original Person, known as Vasudeva and Krishna, who are the beloved Lord and protector of all Your pure devotees."

स्वच्छन्दोपात्तदेहाय विशुद्धज्ञानमूर्तये ।

सर्वस्मै सर्वबीजाय सर्वभूतात्मने नमः ॥10.27.11॥

Word-by-word meaning: स्वच्छन्द-उपात्त-देहाय to Him who assumes a body according to His own sweet will, विशुद्ध-ज्ञान-मूर्तये to the embodiment of pure knowledge, सर्वस्मै to Him who is everything (the totality), सर्व-बीजाय to the seed/source of all, सर्व-भूत-आत्मने to the Soul of all living beings (the Supersoul), नमः obeisances

Anvaya: स्वच्छन्दोपात्तदेहाय विशुद्धज्ञानमूर्तये सर्वस्मै सर्वबीजाय सर्वभूतात्मने नमः।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: स्वच्छन्दोपात्तदेहाय Unto Him who assumes a transcendental form by His own independent will, विशुद्धज्ञानमूर्तये who is the very embodiment of pure, absolute knowledge, सर्वस्मै who is the manifest universe, सर्वबीजाय who is the original seed of everything, सर्वभूतात्मने नमः and who is the indwelling Soul of all living entities, I offer my obeisances.

Simple Meaning: "I offer my respectful obeisances unto You, who assume Your divine form entirely by Your own choice and pleasure. You are the personification of pure, transcendental knowledge. You are the source of everything that exists, the original seed from which all creation springs, and the Supersoul residing in the hearts of every living being."

मयेदं भगवन् गोष्ठनाशायासारवायुभिः ।

चेष्टितं विहते यज्ञे मानिना तीव्रमन्युना ॥10.27.12॥

Word-by-word meaning: मया by me, इदम् this (act), भगवन् O Lord, गोष्ठ-नाशाय for the destruction of the cowherd settlement, आसार-वायुभिः by means of torrential rains and fierce winds, चेष्टितम् was attempted, विहते being frustrated/stopped, यज्ञे the sacrifice, मानिना by one who is proud, तीव्र-मन्युना by one possessing intense anger

Anvaya: भगवन्! यज्ञे विहते (सति), मानिना तीव्रमन्युना मया गोष्ठनाशाय आसारवायुभिः इदं चेष्टितम्।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: भगवन्! O Supreme Lord! यज्ञे विहते (सति) When my sacrifice was stayed, मानिना तीव्रमन्युना मया by me—who am full of pride and intense anger— गोष्ठनाशाय आसारवायुभिः इदं चेष्टितम् this attempt was made to destroy the cowherd community by sending torrential rains and heavy winds.

Simple Meaning: "O Lord, when my sacrifice was stopped, my pride was deeply hurt. In a fit of intense anger and arrogance, I attempted to destroy the entire settlement of Vraja by unleashing devastating rains and fierce winds."

त्वयेशानुगृहीतोऽस्मि ध्वस्तस्तम्भो वृथोद्यमः ।

ईश्वरं गुरुमात्मानं त्वामहं शरणं गतः ॥10.27.13॥

Word-by-word meaning: त्वया by You, ईश O Lord, अनुगृहीतः favored/blessed, अस्मि I am, ध्वस्त-स्तम्भः whose false pride has been shattered, वृथा-उद्यमः whose efforts were rendered useless, ईश्वरम् the Supreme Controller, गुरुम् the Spiritual Master, आत्मानम् the Supreme Soul, त्वाम् to You, अहम् I, शरणम् गतः have gone for shelter

Anvaya: ईश! ध्वस्तस्तम्भः वृथोद्यमः (च) अहं त्वया अनुगृहीतः अस्मि। ईश्वरं गुरुम् आत्मानं त्वाम् अहं शरणं गतः।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: ईश! O Lord! ध्वस्तस्तम्भः वृथोद्यमः (च) अहं I, whose pride has been shattered and whose efforts have been made useless, त्वया अनुगृहीतः अस्मि have been favored by You. ईश्वरं गुरुम् आत्मानं त्वाम् To You—the Supreme Controller, the Spiritual Master, and the Supreme Soul— अहं शरणं गतः I have come for shelter.

Simple Meaning: Indra says: "O Lord, I am actually blessed by You. By making my prideful efforts fail, You have shattered my arrogance. Now, recognizing You as the Supreme Controller, the ultimate Teacher, and the very Soul of my soul, I come to You in full surrender. I seek Your protection and shelter alone."

श्री शुक उवाच

एवं संकीर्तितः कृष्णो मघोना भगवानमुम् ।

मेघगम्भीरया वाचा प्रहसन्निदमब्रवीत् ॥10.27.14॥

Word-by-word meaning: श्री शुकः उवाच Shri Shuka said, एवम् thus, संकीर्तितः glorified, कृष्णः Krishna, मघोना by Indra (Maghavan), भगवान् the Supreme Lord, अमुम् to him (Indra), मेघ-गम्भीरया as grave as a rumbling cloud, वाचा with a voice, प्रहसन् smiling broadly, इदम् this, अब्रवीत् spoke

Anvaya: श्री शुकः उवाच – मघोना एवं संकीर्तितः भगवान् कृष्णः प्रहसन् मेघगम्भीरया वाचा अमुम् इदम् अब्रवीत्।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: श्री शुकः उवाच Shri Shuka said, मघोना एवं संकीर्तितः Having been thus glorified by Indra, भगवान् कृष्णः the Supreme Lord Krishna, प्रहसन् smiling broadly, मेघगम्भीरया वाचा with a voice as deep and grave as a rumbling cloud, अमुम् इदम् अब्रवीत् spoke these words to him.

Simple Meaning: Shri Shuka said: After being glorified by Indra in this way, the Supreme Lord Krishna smiled broadly. With a voice that resonated with the deep, grave sound of a rumbling rain cloud, He began to speak to Indra.

श्री भगवान् उवाच

मया तेऽकारि मघवन् मखभङ्गोऽनुगृह्णता ।

मदनुस्मृतये नित्यं मत्तस्येन्द्रश्रिया भृशम् ॥10.27.15॥

Word-by-word meaning: श्री भगवान् उवाच the Supreme Lord said, मया by Me, ते your, अकारि was done, मघवन् O Indra, मख-भङ्गः the stopping (breaking) of the sacrifice, अनुगृह्णता by Me who was showing you favor/mercy, मत्-अनुस्मृतये for the sake of remembering Me, नित्यम् constantly, मत्तस्य of one who was intoxicated, इन्द्र-श्रिया by the opulence of being Indra, भृशम् greatly

अकारि - कृ , णिजन्ते - भावकर्मणोः लुङ्लकारः (आत्मनेपदम्) , प्रथमपुरुषः , एकवचनम् src. Dhatupatha, Ashtadhyayi

Anvaya: श्री भगवान् उवाच – मघवन्! इन्द्रश्रिया भृशं मत्तस्य ते मया अनुगृह्णता (सता) नित्यं मदनुस्मृतये मखभङ्गः अकारि।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: श्री भगवान् उवाच the Supreme Lord said, मघवन्! O Indra! इन्द्रश्रिया भृशं मत्तस्य ते Of you, who were greatly intoxicated by the opulence of your position as Indra, मया अनुगृह्णता (सता) by Me, who was actually bestowing mercy upon you, नित्यं मदनुस्मृतये so that you may constantly remember Me, मखभङ्गः अकारि the disruption of your sacrifice was performed.

Simple Meaning: The Supreme Lord said: "O Indra, you had become deeply intoxicated by your great power and the opulence of your heavenly throne. It was actually out of My mercy that I stopped your sacrifice. I did this so that your false pride would be shattered, allowing you to turn your heart back toward Me and remember Me constantly."

मामैश्वर्यश्रीमदान्धो दण्डपाणिं न पश्यति ।

तं भ्रंशयामि सम्पद्भ्यो यस्य चेच्छाम्यनुग्रहम् ॥10.27.16॥

Word-by-word meaning: माम् Me, ऐश्वर्य-श्री-मद-अन्धः one blinded by the intoxication of power and wealth, दण्ड-पाणिम् wielding the rod of punishment (as the supreme judge), न पश्यति does not see, तम् him, भ्रंशयामि I cause to fall, सम्पद्भ्यः from his opulences/possessions, यस्य of whom, च and, इच्छामि I desire, अनुग्रहम् (to show) favor/mercy

Anvaya: ऐश्वर्यश्रीमदान्धः दण्डपाणिं मां न पश्यति। यस्य अहम् अनुग्रहम् इच्छामि, तं सम्पद्भ्यः भ्रंशयामि।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: ऐश्वर्यश्रीमदान्धः A person blinded by the intoxication of power and wealth, दण्डपाणिं मां न पश्यति does not see Me standing before him as the punisher (Time). यस्य अहम् अनुग्रहम् इच्छामि To whomsoever I desire to show My mercy, तं सम्पद्भ्यः भ्रंशयामि I cause that person to fall from their material opulences.

Simple Meaning: The Lord explains: "When someone is blinded by the pride of wealth and position, they lose sight of Me and My supreme authority. Therefore, when I truly wish to bless someone and bring them back to their senses, I take away the very opulences that caused their blindness. By removing their material possessions, I allow them to see the truth once again."

गम्यतां शक्र भद्रं वः क्रियतां मेऽनुशासनम् ।

स्थीयतां स्वाधिकारेषु युक्तैर्वः स्तम्भवर्जितैः ॥10.27.17॥

Word-by-word meaning: गम्यताम् you may go, शक्र O Indra, भद्रम् all good fortune, वः unto you, क्रियताम् should be carried out, मे My, अनुशासनम् instruction, स्थीयताम् you should remain, स्व-अधिकारेषु in your respective posts/offices, युक्तैः by being disciplined/steadfast, वः you, स्तम्भ-वर्जितैः free from false pride

Anvaya: शक्र! गम्यताम्, वः भद्रं (भूयात्)। मे अनुशासनं क्रियताम्। वः स्तम्भवर्जितैः युक्तैः स्वाधिकारेषु स्थीयताम्।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: शक्र! गम्यताम् O Indra, you may now depart, वः भद्रं (भूयात्) may all good fortune be unto you. मे अनुशासनं क्रियताम् My instructions should be followed. वः स्तम्भवर्जितैः युक्तैः You, becoming free from pride and remaining disciplined, स्वाधिकारेषु स्थीयताम् should stay in your respective positions of authority.

Simple Meaning: Shri Krishna says: "O Indra, you may now return to your heavenly abode. May all good fortune attend you. Follow My instructions and continue to carry out your duties in your designated positions. However, ensure that you remain disciplined and entirely free from the false pride that led to this mistake."

अथाह सुरभिः कृष्णमभिवन्द्य मनस्विनी ।

स्वसन्तानैरुपामन्त्र्य गोपरूपिणमीश्वरम् ॥10.27.18॥

Word-by-word meaning: अथ then, आह spoke, सुरभिः Mother Surabhi (the celestial cow), कृष्णम् to Krishna, अभिवन्द्य having offered respects/bowed down, मनस्विनी the wise/virtuous one, स्व-सन्तानैः along with her offspring (the cows), उपामन्त्र्य having addressed, गोप-रूपिणम् appearing in the form of a cowherd, ईश्वरम् the Supreme Lord

Anvaya: अथ मनस्विनी सुरभिः स्वसन्तानैः (सह) अभिवन्द्य गोपरूपिणम् ईश्वरं कृष्णम् उपामन्त्र्य आह।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: अथ मनस्विनी सुरभिः Then, the wise and virtuous Surabhi, स्वसन्तानैः (सह) along with her offspring, the cows, अभिवन्द्य having offered her humble respects, गोपरूपिणम् ईश्वरं कृष्णम् उपामन्त्र्य addressing Lord Krishna, who was appearing in the form of a cowherd but is the Supreme Controller, आह spoke.

Simple Meaning: Then, the wise Mother Surabhi, accompanied by her progeny, the cows, bowed down before Shri Krishna. Although He was appearing in the simple role of a cowherd boy of Vraja, she addressed Him with deep reverence as the Supreme Controller of all existence and began to speak.

सुरभिः उवाच

कृष्ण कृष्ण महायोगिन् विश्वात्मन् विश्वसम्भव ।

भवता लोकनाथेन सनाथा वयमच्युत ॥10.27.19॥

Word-by-word meaning: सुरभिः उवाच Surabhi said, कृष्ण कृष्ण O Krishna, O Krishna, महा-योगिन् O master of all mystic power, विश्व-आत्मन् O Soul of the universe, विश्व-सम्भव O source of the universe, भवता by You, लोक-नाथेन the Lord of the worlds, स-नाथाः having a protector/master, वयम् we, अच्युत O infallible one

Anvaya: सुरभिः उवाच –  कृष्ण कृष्ण! महायोगिन्! विश्वात्मन्! विश्वसम्भव! अच्युत! लोकनाथेन भवता वयं सनाथाः (स्मः)।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: सुरभिः उवाच Surabhi said, कृष्ण कृष्ण! महायोगिन्! O Krishna, Krishna! O greatest of all yogis! विश्वात्मन्! विश्वसम्भव! O Soul of the universe and source of all creation! अच्युत! O infallible Lord! लोकनाथेन भवता By You, who are the true master of all the worlds, वयं सनाथाः (स्मः) we have finally found our true protector.

Simple Meaning: Mother Surabhi prays: "O Krishna, O Master of all mystic power! You are the very Soul of the universe and the source from which everything springs. O Infallible One, although the world may look to others for leadership, we recognize that You are the only true Lord. With You as our Master, we now feel truly protected and whole."

त्वं नः परमकं दैवं त्वं न इन्द्रो जगत्पते ।

भवाय भव गोविप्रदेवानां ये च साधवः ॥10.27.20॥

Word-by-word meaning: त्वम् You, नः our, परमकम् supreme, दैवम् Worshipable Deity, त्वम् You, नः our, इन्द्रः Indra (king/leader), जगत्-पते O Lord of the universe, भवाय for the prosperity/well-being, भव please become, गो-विप्र-देवानाम् of the cows, the brahmanas, and the demigods, ये those who, च and, साधवः saintly persons

Anvaya: जगत्पते! त्वं नः परमकं दैवं (असि), त्वं नः इन्द्रः (असि) । गोविप्रदेवानां ये च साधवः (सन्ति, तेषां) भवाय भव।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: जगत्पते! O Lord of the universe! त्वं नः परमकं दैवं (असि) You are our supreme Worshipable Deity, त्वं नः इन्द्रः (असि) and You are our true Indra (King). गोविप्रदेवानां For the cows, the brahmanas, and the demigods, ये च साधवः (सन्ति, तेषां) भवाय भव and for all those who are saintly, please become the source of prosperity and protection.

Simple Meaning: Mother Surabhi says: "O Master of the universe! You are our supreme Deity and our true King. You are our true Indra (King). We beg You to act as our leader for the welfare and prosperity of the cows, the learned brahmanas, the demigods, and all saintly souls who seek shelter in righteousness."

इन्द्रं नस्त्वाभिषेक्ष्यामो ब्रह्मणा नोदिता वयम् ।

अवतीर्णोऽसि विश्वात्मन् भूमेर्भारापनुत्तये ॥10.27.21॥

Word-by-word meaning: इन्द्रम् as King (Indra), नः our, त्वा You, अभिषेक्ष्यामः we shall perform the bathing ceremony (coronation), ब्रह्मणा by Lord Brahma, नोदिताः inspired/ordered, वयम् we, अवतीर्णः असि You have descended, विश्वात्मन् O Soul of the universe, भूमेः of the earth, भार-अपनुत्तये for the removal of the burden

Anvaya: विश्वात्मन्! वयं ब्रह्मणा नोदिताः (सन्तः) त्वा नः इन्द्रम् अभिषेक्ष्यामः। (यतः त्वं) भूमेः भारापनुत्तये अवतीर्णः असि।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: विश्वात्मन्! O Soul of the universe! वयं ब्रह्मणा नोदिताः (सन्तः) We, having been requested and inspired by Lord Brahma, त्वा नः इन्द्रम् अभिषेक्ष्यामः shall now perform Your coronation as our Indra (King). (यतः त्वं) भूमेः भारापनुत्तये अवतीर्णः असि For You have descended to this world specifically to remove the heavy burden of the earth.

Simple Meaning: Mother Surabhi continues: "O Soul of the Universe! Lord Brahma himself has inspired and instructed us to install You as our true King. We have come to perform Your coronation ceremony. We recognize that You have appeared in this world to relieve the earth of its heavy burden and to protect all living beings as our ultimate Lord."

श्री शुक उवाच

एवं कृष्णमुपामन्त्र्य सुरभिः पयसाऽऽत्मनः ।

जलैराकाशगङ्गाया ऐरावतकरोद्धृतैः ॥10.27.22॥

Word-by-word meaning: श्री शुकः उवाच Shri Shuka said, एवम् thus, कृष्णम् Krishna, उपामन्त्र्य having addressed/invited, सुरभिः Mother Surabhi, पयसा with milk, आत्मनः her own, जलैः with the waters, आकाश-गङ्गायाः of the celestial Ganges (Akasha-ganga), ऐरावत-कर-उद्धृतैः carried in the trunk of the elephant Airavata

Anvaya: श्री शुकः उवाच— एवं कृष्णम् उपामन्त्र्य, सुरभिः आत्मनः पयसा, (इन्द्रः च) ऐरावतकरोद्धृतैः आकाशगङ्गायाः जलैः (अभिषिषिचतुः)।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: श्री शुकः उवाच Shri Shuka said— एवं कृष्णम् उपामन्त्र्य having thus addressed Lord Krishna, सुरभिः आत्मनः पयसा Mother Surabhi with her own milk, (इन्द्रः च) ऐरावतकरोद्धृतैः आकाशगङ्गायाः जलैः and Indra with the waters of the heavenly Ganges carried in the trunk of his elephant Airavata, (अभिषिषिचतुः) bathed Him.

Simple Meaning: Shri Shuka said: After addressing Lord Krishna in this way, Mother Surabhi bathed the Lord with her own milk. Simultaneously, Indra, using the trunk of his celestial elephant Airavata, poured the sacred waters of the heavenly Ganges over the Lord, performing His coronation bath.

इन्द्र सुरर्षिभिः साकं नोदितो देवमातृभिः ।

अभ्यषिञ्चत दाशार्हं गोविन्द इति चाभ्यधात् ॥10.27.23॥

Word-by-word meaning: इन्द्रः Indra, सुर-ऋषिभिः साकम् along with the celestial sages (like Narada and others), नोदितः inspired/urged, देव-मातृभिः by the mothers of the demigods (like Aditi), अभ्यषिञ्चत performed the ritual bath (coronation), दाशार्हम् unto the descendant of Dasharha (Krishna), गोविन्दः Govinda, इति thus, च and, अभ्यधात् named/proclaimed

Anvaya: इन्द्रः सुरर्षिभिः देवमातृभिः (च) साकं नोदितः (सन्) दाशार्हम् अभ्यषिञ्चत, ‘गोविन्दः’ इति च अभ्यधात्।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: इन्द्रः सुरर्षिभिः देवमातृभिः (च) साकं नोदितः (सन्) Indra, being urged and accompanied by the celestial sages and the mothers of the demigods, दाशार्हम् अभ्यषिञ्चत performed the ceremonial bath of Krishna (the descendant of Dasharha), ‘गोविन्दः’ इति च अभ्यधात् and proclaimed Him by the name "Govinda."

Simple Meaning: Inspired by the celestial sages and the divine mothers of the heavens, Indra performed the sacred coronation of Lord Krishna. As he bathed the Lord, he formally announced His new name: Govinda, which means "He who gives pleasure to the cows, the land, and the senses," or "the Lord of the cows."

The Significance of the Name "Govinda"

This name is deeply symbolic in this context:

By giving Him this name, Indra publicly admits that Krishna is the true "Indra" (leader) of the cows and the entire spiritual landscape of Vraja.

तत्रागतास्तुम्बुरुनारदादयो गन्धर्वविद्याधरसिद्धचारणाः ।

जगुर्यशो लोकमलापहं हरेः सुराङ्गनाः संननृतुर्मुदान्विताः ॥10.27.24॥

Word-by-word meaning: तत्र there (at the site of the coronation), आगताः having arrived, तुम्बुरु-नारद-आदयः Tumburu, Narada, and others, गन्धर्व-विद्याधर-सिद्ध-चारणाः the Gandharvas (celestial musicians), Vidyadharas, Siddhas, and Charanas (heavenly singers), जगुः sang, यशः the glories/fame, लोक-मल-अपहम् which removes the impurities of the world, हरेः of Lord Hari (Krishna), सुर-अङ्गनाः the celestial women (Apsaras), संननृतुः danced beautifully/collectively, मुदा-अन्विताः filled with great joy

Anvaya: तत्र आगताः तुम्बुरुनारदादयः गन्धर्वविद्याधरसिद्धचारणाः हरेः लोकमलापहं यशः जगुः, मुदान्विताः सुराङ्गनाः (च) संननृतुः।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: तत्र आगताः तुम्बुरुनारदादयः Having arrived there, Narada, Tumburu, and other great sages, गन्धर्वविद्याधरसिद्धचारणाः along with the Gandharvas, Vidyadharas, Siddhas, and Charanas, हरेः लोकमलापहं यशः जगुः sang the glories of Lord Hari, which wash away all the sins and impurities of the world. मुदान्विताः सुराङ्गनाः (च) संननृतुः Filled with bliss, the celestial damsels danced in celebration.

Simple Meaning: The entire sky filled with divine beings. Great sages like Narada and Tumburu, along with celestial musicians and perfected beings, began to sing the glories of Lord Krishna—glories that have the power to purify the entire world of its sins. Filled with immense joy, the Apsaras and heavenly women danced to the rhythm of the divine music, celebrating the coronation of the true Lord of the universe.

Spiritual Significance: The phrase "Loka-mala-apaham" (removing the impurities of the world) is significant here. It suggests that Krishna's pastime of humbling Indra and being named Govinda is not just a historical event, but a cleansing force for anyone who hears or sings it, removing the "dirt" of material pride and ego from the heart.

तं तुष्टुवुर्देवनिकायकेतवो व्यवाकिरंचाद्भुतपुष्पवृष्टिभिः ।

लोकाः परां निर्वृतिमाप्नुवंस्त्रयो गावस्तदा गामनयन् पयोद्रुताम् ॥10.27.25॥

Word-by-word meaning: तम् Him (Krishna), तुष्टुवुः praised/offered prayers, देव-निकाय-केतवः the eminent leaders of the assembly of demigods, व्यवाकिरन् they showered/scattered, च and, अद्भुत-पुष्प-वृष्टिभिः with wonderful showers of flowers, लोकाः the worlds, पराम् supreme, निर्वृतिम् satisfaction/peace, आप्नुवन् attained, त्रयः the three (worlds), गावः the cows, तदा then, गाम् the earth, अनयन् brought to/transformed, पयः-द्रुताम् flooded with milk

Anvaya: देवनिकायकेतवः तं तुष्टुवुः अद्भुतपुष्पवृष्टिभिः च व्यवाकिरन्। तदा त्रयः लोकाः परां निर्वृतिम् आप्नुवन्, गावः (च) गां पयःद्रुताम् अनयन्।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: देवनिकायकेतवः तं तुष्टुवुः The chief leaders of the demigods offered prayers to Him, अद्भुतपुष्पवृष्टिभिः च व्यवाकिरन् and showered Him with wonderful rains of celestial flowers. तदा त्रयः लोकाः परां निर्वृतिम् आप्नुवन् At that time, all the three worlds attained supreme peace and satisfaction, गावः (च) गां पयःद्रुताम् अनयन् and the cows flooded the entire earth with their milk.

Simple Meaning: The most prominent leaders of the demigods offered heartfelt prayers to Lord Krishna and showered Him with a magnificent rain of celestial flowers. A deep sense of peace and spiritual satisfaction spread across the three worlds. The joy was so great that the cows of Vraja let down their milk so freely that the very ground was soaked and the earth appeared to be flooded with milk.

नानारसौघाः सरितो वृक्षा आसन् मधुस्रवाः ।

अकृष्टपच्यौषधयो गिरयोऽबिभ्रदुन्मणीन् ॥10.27.26॥

Word-by-word meaning: नाना-रस-ओघाः (possessing) flows of various tasty liquids/juices, सरितः the rivers, वृक्षाः the trees, आसन् became, मधु-स्रवाः dripping with honey/sweet sap, अकृष्ट-पच्याः ripening without cultivation (spontaneously), ओषधयः the plants/herbs, गिरयः the mountains, अबिभ्रत् exhibited/held forth, उन्मणीन् precious jewels/shining gems

Anvaya: सरितः नानारसौघाः (आसन्), वृक्षाः मधुस्रवाः आसन्, ओषधयः अकृष्टपच्याः (आसन्), गिरयः (च) उन्मणीन् अबिभ्रत्।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: सरितः नानारसौघाः (आसन्) The rivers became flows of various delicious juices/tastes; वृक्षाः मधुस्रवाः आसन् the trees began to drip with sweet honey; ओषधयः अकृष्टपच्याः (आसन्) the food plants and herbs ripened spontaneously without being plowed or cultivated; गिरयः (च) उन्मणीन् अबिभ्रत् and the mountains manifested and offered up precious, shining jewels.

Simple Meaning: At the time of Krishna’s coronation, all of nature rejoiced. The rivers began to flow with various delicious juices, and the trees started dripping with sweet honey. The earth became so fertile that crops and medicinal herbs ripened on their own without needing any labor or plowing. Even the mountains, moved by the divine atmosphere, revealed their hidden treasures of brilliant, shining gems.

कृष्णेऽभिषिक्त एतानि सत्त्वानि कुरुनन्दन ।

निर्वैराण्यभवंस्तात क्रूराण्यपि निसर्गतः॥10.27.27॥

Word-by-word meaning: कृष्णे in Krishna, अभिषिक्ते having been bathed/consecrated (coronated), एतानि these, सत्त्वानि living beings, कुरु-नन्दन O delight of the Kurus (Parikshit), निर्वैराणि free from enmity/hostility, अभवन् became, तात O dear one, क्रूराणि cruel/predatory, अपि even, निसर्गतः by nature

Anvaya: कुरुनन्दन! तात! कृष्णे अभिषिक्ते (सति), निसर्गतः क्रूराणि अपि एतानि सत्त्वानि निर्वैराणि अभवन्।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: कुरुनन्दन! तात! O beloved descendant of the Kurus! कृष्णे अभिषिक्ते (सति) When Krishna was thus coronated, निसर्गतः क्रूराणि अपि even those who are cruel by their very nature, एतानि सत्त्वानि निर्वैराणि अभवन् these living beings became completely free from enmity.

Simple Meaning: O beloved prince of the Kurus, when Krishna was crowned as Govinda, a miracle occurred in the hearts of all creatures. Even those animals that are natural enemies or predatory by instinct—like the snake and the eagle, or the lion and the deer—completely forgot their cruelty and lived together in perfect, peaceful harmony.

इति गोगोकुलपतिं गोविन्दमभिषिच्य सः: ।

अनुज्ञातो ययौ शक्रो वृतो देवादिभिर्दिवम् ॥10.27.28॥

Word-by-word meaning: इति thus, गो-गोकुल-पतिम् the Master of the cows and the community of cowherds, गोविन्दम् Lord Govinda, अभिषिच्य having performed the coronation bath, सः he, शक्रः Indra, अनुज्ञातः having received permission, ययौ went, वृतः surrounded/accompanied, देव-आदिभिः by the demigods and others, दिवम् to the heavenly planets

Anvaya: इति सः शक्रः गोगोकुलपतिं गोविन्दम् अभिषिच्य, अनुज्ञातः (सन्), देवादिभिः वृतः दिवं ययौ।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: इति सः शक्रः In this way, that Indra, गोगोकुलपतिं गोविन्दम् अभिषिच्य having coronated Govinda, the Master of the cows and the cowherd community, अनुज्ञातः (सन्) and having received permission (to leave), देवादिभिः वृतः दिवं ययौ departed for the heavens surrounded by the demigods and other celestial beings

Simple Meaning: Thus, after coronating Lord Govinda as the supreme Master of the cows and all of Vraja, Indra took leave of the Lord. With Krishna's permission, he returned to his heavenly abode, accompanied by the assembly of demigods and celestial sages who had gathered for the festival.

इति श्रीमद्भागवते महापुराणे पारमहंस्यां संहितायां दशमस्कन्धे पूर्वार्धे “इन्द्रस्तुतिर्नाम नाम” सप्तविंशः अध्यायः

 ॥27॥

Thus ends the twenty-seventh discourse named 'Indra extols Shri Krishna,' in the first half of Book Ten of the great and glorious Bhagavata-Purana.
