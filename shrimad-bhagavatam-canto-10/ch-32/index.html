Shrimad Bhagwatam - Book 1

Canto 10

Chapter 32

The Lord comforts the Gopis during the Rasa Play

श्रीमद्भागवतमहापुराणम्

दशमः स्कन्धः

रासक्रीडायां गोपीसान्त्वनं नाम द्वात्रिंशः अध्यायः

(22 Shlokas)

श्री शुक उवाच

इति गोप्यः प्रगायन्त्यः प्रलपन्त्यश्च चित्रधा ।

रुरुदुः सुस्वरं राजन् कृष्णदर्शनलालसाः ॥10.32.1॥

Word-by-word meaning: श्री शुकः उवाच Shri Shuka said, इति thus, गोप्यः the gopis, प्रगायन्त्यः singing loudly, प्रलपन्त्यः speaking incoherently/lamenting, च and, चित्रधा in various ways, रुरुदुः cried, सु-स्वरम् loudly/pitifully, राजन् O King, कृष्ण-दर्शन-लालसाः greatly desiring the vision of Krishna

Anvaya: श्री शुकः उवाच — राजन्! इति कृष्णदर्शनलालसाः गोप्यः चित्रधा प्रगायन्त्यः प्रलपन्त्यः च सुस्वरं रुरुदुः।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: श्री शुकः उवाच Shri Shuka said, राजन् O King, इति thus, कृष्णदर्शनलालसाः greatly desiring the vision of Krishna, गोप्यः the gopis, चित्रधा in various ways, प्रगायन्त्यः singing, प्रलपन्त्यः च and lamenting, सुस्वरं रुरुदुः cried out loudly.

Simple Meaning: Shri Shuka said: O King, having thus sung and spoken of Krishna in various distraught ways, the gopis, who were intensely longing to see Him, began to cry out piteously in loud voices.

तासामाविरभूच्छौरिः स्मयमानमुखाम्बुजः ।

पीताम्बरधरः स्रग्वी साक्षान्मन्मथमन्मथः ॥10.32.2॥

Word-by-word meaning: तासाम् amidst them, आविरभूत् appeared, शौरिः Lord Krishna (the descendant of Shura), स्मयमान-मुख-अम्बुजः His lotus-like face smiling, पीत-अम्बर-धरः wearing yellow garments, स्रग्वी wearing a flower garland, साक्षात् directly, मन्मथ-मन्मथः the bewilderer of the mind of Cupid

मन्मथस्य मन्मथः = मन्मथमन्मथः षष्ठी-तत्पुरुषः समासः

Anvaya: तासां स्मयमानमुखाम्बुजः पीताम्बरधरः स्रग्वी साक्षान्मन्मथमन्मथः शौरिः आविरभूत्।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: तासां Amidst them, स्मयमानमुखाम्बुजः  whose lotus face was smiling, पीताम्बरधरः  wearing yellow silken clothes, स्रग्वी wearing a garland, साक्षात् मन्मथमन्मथः who is directly the Enchanter of the enchanter (Cupid), शौरिः आविरभूत् Lord Krishna appeared.

Simple Meaning: Lord Krishna then appeared before them with a gentle smile on His lotus-like face. Wearing His yellow silk garments and a flower garland, He appeared so beautiful that He directly bewildered the mind of Cupid, who himself bewilders the world.

तं विलोक्यागतं प्रेष्ठं प्रीत्युत्फुल्लदृशोऽबलाः ।

उत्तस्थुर्युगपत् सर्वास्तन्वः प्राणीनामिवागतम् ॥10.32.3॥

Word-by-word meaning: तम् Him, विलोक्य seeing, आगतम् arrived, प्रेष्ठम् the most beloved, प्रीति-उत्फुल्ल-दृशः eyes opening wide with affection, अबलाः the gopis (women), उत्तस्थुः stood up, युगपत् simultaneously, सर्वाः all of them, तन्वः the limbs/bodies, प्राणम् life breath, इव like, आगतम् returned

Anvaya: तं प्रेष्ठम् आगतं विलोक्य प्रीत्युत्फुल्लदृशः सर्वाः अबलाः, प्राणम् आगतं तन्वः इव, युगपत् उत्तस्थुः।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: तं प्रेष्ठम् आगतं विलोक्य Seeing that most beloved one had arrived, प्रीत्युत्फुल्लदृशः their eyes blooming with joy, सर्वाः अबलाः all the gopis, प्राणम् आगतं तन्वः इव like the limbs of a body when life breath returns, युगपत् उत्तस्थुः stood up all at once.

Simple Meaning: As soon as the gopis saw that their most beloved Krishna had returned, their eyes blossomed with joy. They all stood up together immediately, just as the limbs of a body regain their movement when the life breath returns to them.

काचित् कराम्बुजं शौरेर्जगृहेऽञ्जलिना मुदा ।

काचिद् दधार तद्बाहुमंसे चन्दनरूषितम् ॥10.32.4॥

Word-by-word meaning: काचित् one gopi, कर-अम्बुजम् the lotus-like hand, शौरेः of Shauri (Krishna), जगृहे took/seized, अञ्जलिना with joined palms, मुदा with joy, काचित् another gopi, दधार placed/held, तत्-बाहुम् His arm, अंसे on her shoulder, चन्दन-रूषितम् smeared with sandalwood paste

Anvaya: काचित् मुदा शौरेः कराम्बुजम् अञ्जलिना जगृहे, काचित् चन्दनरूषितं तद्बाहुम् अंसे दधार।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: काचित् मुदा One gopi with joy, शौरेः कराम्बुजम् the lotus-like hand of Krishna, अञ्जलिना जगृहे took within her joined palms, काचित् another gopi, चन्दनरूषितं तद्बाहुम् His arm which was smeared with sandalwood paste, अंसे दधार placed on her shoulder.

Simple Meaning: One of the gopis, filled with joy, took Krishna's lotus-like hand between her joined palms. Another gopi placed His arm, which was fragrant and smeared with sandalwood paste, upon her shoulder.

काचिदञ्जलिनागृह्णात्तन्वी ताम्बूलचर्वितम् ।

एका तदङ्घ्रिकमलं सन्तप्ता स्तनयोरधात् ॥10.32.5॥

Word-by-word meaning: काचित् one gopi, अञ्जलिना with joined palms, अगृह्णात् took, तन्वी the slender girl, ताम्बूल-चर्वितम् the chewed betel nut (remnants), एका another, तत्-अङ्घ्रि-कमलम् His lotus feet, सन्तप्ता burning with distress, स्तनयोः on her breasts, अधात् placed

Anvaya: काचित् तन्वी ताम्बूलचर्वितम् अञ्जलिना अगृह्णात्, एका सन्तप्ता तदङ्घ्रिकमलं स्तनयोः अधात्।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: काचित् तन्वी One slender gopi, ताम्बूलचर्वितम् the chewed betel nut remnants, अञ्जलिना अगृह्णात् took with her joined palms, एका सन्तप्ता another who was burning with distress, तदङ्घ्रिकमलं His lotus feet, स्तनयोः अधात् placed on her breasts.

Simple Meaning: One slender gopi took the remnants of the betel nut Krishna had chewed into her joined palms. Another gopi, burning with the heat of separation, took His lotus feet and placed them upon her heart to soothe her distress.

एका भ्रुकुटिमाबध्य प्रेमसंरम्भविह्वला ।

घ्नतीवैक्षत् कटाक्षेपैः संदष्टदशनच्छदा ॥10.32.6॥

Word-by-word meaning: एका one gopi, भ्रुकुटिम् her eyebrows, आबध्य knitting/contracting, प्रेम-संरम्भ-विह्वला overwhelmed by the fury of love, घ्नती striking/biting, इव as if, ऐक्षत् looked at, कटाक्ष-क्षेपैः with side-long glances, संदष्ट-दशन-च्छदा biting her lip with her teeth

Anvaya: प्रेमसंरम्भविह्वला एका भ्रुकुटिमाबध्य संदष्टदशनच्छदा कटाक्षेपैः घ्नती इव ऐक्षत्।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: प्रेमसंरम्भविह्वला एका One gopi overwhelmed by the fury of love, भ्रुकुटिमाबध्य knitting her eyebrows, संदष्टदशनच्छदा biting her lip with her teeth, कटाक्षेपैः with side-long glances, घ्नती इव ऐक्षत् looked at Him as if striking Him.

Simple Meaning: One gopi, overwhelmed by a state of loving anger, knit her eyebrows and bit her lip with her teeth. She glared at Krishna with sidelong glances as if she were striking Him with her eyes.

अपरानिमिषद्दृग्भ्यां जुषाणा तन्मुखाम्बुजम् ।

आपीतमपि नातृप्यत् सन्तस्तच्चरणं यथा॥10.32.7॥

Word-by-word meaning: अपरा another gopi, अनिमिषत्-दृग्भ्याम् with unblinking eyes, जुषाणा relishing/honoring, तत्-मुख-अम्बुजम् His lotus-like face, आपीतम् having fully drunk (the nectar), अपि even though, न not, अतृप्यत् felt satiated, सन्तः saintly persons, तत्-चरणम् His feet, यथा just as

Anvaya: अपरा अनिमिषद्दृग्भ्यां तन्मुखाम्बुजं जुषाणा आपीतम् अपि न अतृप्यत्, यथा सन्तः तच्चरणम्।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: अपरा Another gopi, अनिमिषद्दृग्भ्यां with unblinking eyes, तन्मुखाम्बुजं जुषाणा relishing His lotus face, आपीतम् अपि even though she had fully drunk the nectar, न अतृप्यत् did not feel satiated, यथा सन्तः तच्चरणम् just as saintly persons are never satiated meditating on His feet.

Simple Meaning: Another gopi looked upon His lotus face with unblinking eyes. Though she drank the nectar of His beauty deeply and repeatedly, she did not feel satisfied, just as saintly devotees are never satiated while meditating upon the Lord's lotus feet.

तं काचिन्नेत्ररन्ध्रेण हृदिकृत्य निमील्य च ।

पुलकाङ्ग्युपगुह्यास्ते योगीवानन्द सम्प्लुता ॥10.32.8॥

Word-by-word meaning: तम् Him, काचित् one gopi, नेत्र-रन्ध्रेण through the aperture of her eyes, हृदि-कृत्य placing Him within her heart, निमील्य closing (her eyes), च and, पुलक-अङ्गी her body's hair standing on end, उपगुह्य embracing (Him), आस्ते remained, योगी a yogi, इव like, आनन्द-सम्प्लुता immersed in an ocean of bliss

Anvaya: काचित् तं नेत्ररन्ध्रेण हृदिकृत्य निमील्य उपगुह्य च पुलकाङ्गी आनन्दसम्प्लुता योगी इव आस्ते।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: काचित् One gopi, तं नेत्ररन्ध्रेण हृदिकृत्य placing Him within her heart through the aperture of her eyes, निमील्य closing them, उपगुह्य च and embracing Him internally, पुलकाङ्गी her hair standing on end, आनन्दसम्प्लुता immersed in an ocean of bliss, योगी इव आस्ते remained like a yogi in meditation.

Simple Meaning: One gopi took Krishna into her heart through the pathway of her eyes. Closing her eyes, she embraced Him internally. With her hair standing on end, submerged in an ocean of spiritual ecstasy, she remained as still as a yogi lost in deep meditation.

सर्वास्ताः केशवालोकपरमोत्सवनिर्वृताः ।

जहुर्विरहजं तापं प्राज्ञं प्राप्य यथा जनाः ॥10.32.9॥

Word-by-word meaning: सर्वाः all, ताः those (gopis), केशव-आलोक-परम-उत्सव-निर्वृताः happy due to the great festival of seeing Keshava, जहुः they gave up, विरह-जम् born of separation, तापम् the distress, प्राज्ञम् a spiritually wise person, प्राप्य having attained, यथा just as, जनाः people.

Anvaya: केशवालोकपरमोत्सवनिर्वृताः ताः सर्वाः विरहजं तापं जहुः, यथा प्राज्ञं प्राप्य जनाः।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: केशवालोकपरमोत्सवनिर्वृताः Fully satisfied by the great festival of seeing Keshava, ताः सर्वाः all of them, विरहजं तापं जहुः gave up the distress born of separation, यथा just as, प्राज्ञं प्राप्य जनाः people give up their miseries upon attaining a spiritually wise person.

Simple Meaning: All the gopis felt as if they were experiencing a grand festival by seeing Keshava again. Just as common people are relieved of all their worldly miseries when they find a realized soul, the gopis were instantly freed from the intense burning agony caused by their separation from Krishna.

ताभिर्विधूतशोकाभिर्भगवानच्युतो वृतः ।

व्यरोचताधिकं तात पुरुषः शक्तिभिर्यथा ॥10.32.10॥

Word-by-word meaning: ताभिः by them, विधूत-शोकाभिः who were freed from their lamentation, भगवान् the Supreme Lord, अच्युतः the infallible Krishna, वृतः surrounded, व्यरोचत shone, अधिकम् exceedingly, तात O dear one (Parikshit), पुरुषः the Supreme Soul, शक्तिभिः by His potencies, यथा just as

Anvaya: तात! विधूतशोकाभिः ताभिः वृतः भगवान् अच्युतः, शक्तिभिः पुरुषः यथा, अधिकं व्यरोचत।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: तात O dear one, विधूतशोकाभिः ताभिः वृतः surrounded by those gopis whose grief had been cast away, भगवान् अच्युतः the infallible Supreme Lord, शक्तिभिः पुरुषः यथा just like the Supreme Soul surrounded by His creative potencies, अधिकं व्यरोचत shone exceedingly.

Simple Meaning: O dear King, surrounded by the gopis who were now free from all sorrow, the infallible Lord Krishna shone with even greater brilliance. He appeared just like the Supreme Personality of Godhead manifest in the company of His original spiritual potencies.

ताः समादाय कालिन्द्या निर्विश्य पुलिनं विभुः ।

विकसत्कुन्दमन्दारसुरभ्यनिलषट्पदम् ॥10.32.11॥

Word-by-word meaning: ताः them (the gopis), समादाय taking along, कालिन्द्याः of the river Yamuna, निर्विश्य entering, पुलिनम् the sandy bank, विभुः the Almighty Lord, विकसत्-कुन्द-मन्दार-सुरभि-अनिल-षट्पदम् where the breeze and bees were made fragrant by blooming Kunda and Mandara flowers

Anvaya: विभुः ताः समादाय कालिन्द्याः विकसत्कुन्दमन्दारसुरभ्यनिलषट्पदंं पुलिनं निर्विश्य (अतिष्ठत)।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: विभुः The Almighty Lord, ताः समादाय taking them along, कालिन्द्याः विकसत्कुन्दमन्दारसुरभ्यनिलषट्पदंं पुलिनं निर्विश्य entered the bank of the Yamuna which was filled with bees and breezes fragrant with the scent of blooming Kunda and Mandara flowers.

Simple Meaning: The Almighty Lord, taking the gopis with Him, went to the bank of the Yamuna. The area was enchanting, with the air and the humming bees carrying the sweet fragrance of blooming Kunda and Mandara flowers.

शरच्चन्द्रांशुसन्दोहध्वस्तदोषातमः शिवम् ।

कृष्णाया हस्ततरलाचितकोमलवालुकम् ॥10.32.12॥

Word-by-word meaning: शरत्-चन्द्र-अंशु-सन्दोह-ध्वस्त-दोषा-तमः where the darkness of night was destroyed by the abundance of rays from the autumn moon, शिवम् auspicious/pleasant, कृष्णायाः of the river Yamuna, हस्त-तरल-आचित-कोमल-वालुकम् whose soft sands had been collected by the wave-like hands of the river

Anvaya: (तत पुलिनं) शरच्चन्द्रांशुसन्दोहध्वस्तदोषातमः शिवं, कृष्णायाः हस्ततरलाचितकोमलवालुकम् (आसीत्)।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: (तत पुलिनं) That riverbank, शरच्चन्द्रांशुसन्दोहध्वस्तदोषातमः where the darkness of the night had been dispelled by the mass of autumn moonlight, शिवं was auspicious, कृष्णायाः हस्ततरलाचितकोमलवालुकम् with soft sands that had been piled up by the wave-hands of the Yamuna.

Simple Meaning: That auspicious bank of the Yamuna was bathed in the thick rays of the autumn moon, which had completely dispelled the darkness of the night. The sand there was soft and clean, having been delicately moved and leveled by the wave-like hands of the river.

तद्दर्शनाह्लादविधूतहृद्रजो मनोरथान्तं श्रुतयो यथा ययुः ।

स्वैरुत्तरीयैः कुचकुङ्कुमाङ्कितैरचीक्लृपन्नासनमात्मबन्धवे ॥10.32.13॥

Word-by-word meaning: तत्-दर्शन-आह्लाद-विधूत-हृद्-रजः the dust of their heart's passion purified by the joy of seeing Him, मनोरथ-अन्तम् the end of all desires (perfection), श्रुतयः the Vedas/Upanishads, यथा just as, ययुः attained, स्वैः with their own, उत्तरीयैः upper garments, कुच-कुङ्कुम्-अङ्कितैः marked with the vermilion (kunkuma) from their breasts, अचीक्लृपन् they prepared, आसनम् a seat, आत्म-बन्धवे for the friend of their soul

Anvaya: तद्दर्शनाह्लादविधूतहृद्रजः (ताः), श्रुतयः यथा मनोरथान्तं ययुः। कुचकुङ्कुमाङ्कितैः स्वैः उत्तरीयैः आत्मबन्धवे आसनम् अचीक्लृपन्।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: तद्दर्शनाह्लादविधूतहृद्रजः Having the dust of passion washed from their hearts by the joy of seeing Him, श्रुतयः यथा मनोरथान्तं ययुः just as the personified Vedas attained the ultimate object of their desire, (they attained fulfillment). कुचकुङ्कुमाङ्कितैः स्वैः उत्तरीयैः With their own upper garments marked with kunkuma from their breasts, आत्मबन्धवे आसनम् अचीक्लृपन् they prepared a seat for the friend of their soul.

Simple Meaning: The joy of seeing Krishna completely washed away any remaining worldly passion from the hearts of the gopis. Just as the personified Vedas attain the ultimate goal of their quest by realizing the Supreme, the gopis reached the perfection of all their desires. They then took their own soft veils, which were marked with the kunkuma from their bodies, and spread them out to create a seat for Krishna, the true friend of their souls.

तत्रोपविष्टो भगवान् स ईश्वरो योगेश्वरान्तर्हृदि कल्पितासनः।

चकास गोपीपरिषद्गतोऽर्चितस्त्रैलोक्यलक्ष्म्येकपदं वपुर्दधत् ॥10.32.14॥

Word-by-word meaning: तत्र there, उपविष्टः seated, भगवान् the Supreme Lord, सः He, ईश्वरः the Controller, योगेश्वर-अन्तर्-हृदि-कल्पित-आसनः who has His seat prepared within the hearts of the masters of yoga, चकास shone, गोपी-परिषत्-गतः being in the assembly of the gopis, अर्चितः worshipped, त्रैलोक्य-लक्ष्मी-एक-पदम् the exclusive abode of all the beauty of the three worlds, वपुः a body, दधत् manifesting

Anvaya: योगेश्वरान्तर्हृदिकल्पितासनः सः ईश्वरः भगवान् तत्र उपविष्टः, गोपीपरिषद्गतः अर्चितः त्रैलोक्यलक्ष्म्येकपदं वपुः दधत् चकास।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: योगेश्वरान्तर्हृदिकल्पितासनः He whose seat is prepared within the hearts of the greatest yogis, सः ईश्वरः भगवान् that Supreme Personality and Controller, तत्र उपविष्टः seated there (on the garments), गोपीपरिषद्गतः अर्चितः being worshipped in the assembly of the gopis, त्रैलोक्यलक्ष्म्येकपदं वपुः दधत् manifesting a form that is the sole repository of all the beauty of the three worlds, चकास shone brilliantly.

Simple Meaning: The Supreme Lord and Controller, who usually sits upon the meditative thrones prepared within the hearts of great masters of yoga, now sat upon the silk garments provided by the gopis. Worshipped by them in their assembly, He shone with magnificent luster, manifesting a form that concentrated within itself the entire beauty and opulence of the three worlds.

सभाजयित्वा तमनङ्गदीपनं सहासलीलेक्षणविभ्रमभ्रुवा ।

संस्पर्शनेनाङ्ककृताङ्घ्रिहस्तयोः संस्तुत्य ईषत्कुपिता बभाषिरे ॥10.32.15॥

Word-by-word meaning: सभाजयित्वा honoring/welcoming, तम् Him, अनङ्ग-दीपनम् the arouser of Cupid (love), स-हास-लीला-ईक्षण-विभ्रम-भ्रुवा with smiling, playful glances and the movement of their eyebrows, संस्पर्शनेन by the touching, अङ्क-कृत-अङ्घ्रि-हस्तयोः of His feet and hands placed in their laps, संस्तुत्य praising, ईषत्-कुपिताः somewhat angry, बभाषिरे they spoke

Anvaya: अङ्ककृताङ्घ्रिहस्तयोः संस्पर्शनेन सहासलीलेक्षणविभ्रमभ्रुवा अनङ्गदीपनं तं सभाजयित्वा संस्तुत्य च, ईषत्कुपिताः (गोप्यः) बभाषिरे।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: अङ्ककृताङ्घ्रिहस्तयोः संस्पर्शनेन By the touch of His feet and hands which they had placed in their laps, सहासलीलेक्षणविभ्रमभ्रुवा with smiling, playful glances and the movement of their eyebrows, अनङ्गदीपनं तं सभाजयित्वा honoring Him who increases their love, संस्तुत्य च and having praised Him, ईषत्कुपिताः बभाषिरे they spoke while feeling somewhat angry.

Simple Meaning: The gopis welcomed Krishna, who fans the fires of their love, by taking His hands and feet into their laps and massaging them. While honoring Him with smiling, playful glances and arched eyebrows, they offered Him praise; yet, feeling a little resentful due to His recent disappearance, they began to speak to Him with an air of loving anger.

गोप्य ऊचुः

भजतोऽनुभजन्त्येक एक एतद्विपर्ययम् ।

नोभयांश्च भजन्त्येक एतन्नो ब्रुहि साधु भोः ॥10.32.16॥

Word-by-word meaning: गोप्यः ऊचुः the gopis said, भजतः to those who acknowledge/love them, अनुभजन्ति reciprocate/love in return, एके some, एके some, एतत् this, विपर्ययम् the opposite, न not, उभयान् both, च and, भजन्ति acknowledge/love, एके some, एतत् this, नः to us, ब्रुहि speak/explain, साधु clearly/truthfully, भोः O (Lord)

Anvaya: गोप्यः ऊचुः — एके भजतः अनुभजन्ति, एके एतद्विपर्ययम् (भजन्ति), एके उभयान् च न भजन्ति; भोः! एतत् नः साधु ब्रुहि।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: गोप्यः ऊचुः The gopis said, एके भजतः अनुभजन्ति some reciprocate only with those who love them, एके एतद्विपर्ययम् some do the opposite (love those who do not love them), एके उभयान् च न भजन्ति and some do not love either category; भोः एतत् नः साधु ब्रुहि O Lord, please explain this clearly to us.

Simple Meaning: The gopis said: Some people reciprocate love only when they are loved first. Others do the opposite, showing love even to those who do not love them. Then there are those who do not acknowledge anyone, whether they are loved or not. O Krishna, please explain the nature of these different types of people to us clearly.

श्री भगवान् उवाच

मिथो भजन्ति ये सख्यः स्वार्थैकान्तोद्यमा हि ते ।

न तत्र सौहृदं धर्मः स्वार्थार्थ तध्हि नान्यथा ॥10.32.17॥

Word-by-word meaning: श्री भगवान् उवाच the Supreme Lord said, मिथः mutually, भजन्ति reciprocate/love, ये those who, सख्यः O friends, स्वार्थ-एकान्त-उद्यमाः whose efforts are solely for their own self-interest, हि indeed, ते they, न not, तत्र there, सौहृदम् true friendship, धर्मः virtue/righteousness, स्वार्थ-अर्थम् for the sake of self-interest, तत् that, हि certainly, न not, अन्यथा otherwise

Anvaya: श्री भगवान् उवाच — (हे) सख्यः! ये मिथः भजन्ति, ते हि स्वार्थैकान्तोद्यमाः (सन्ति)। तत्र सौहृदं धर्मः (वा) न (अस्ति), तत् हि स्वार्थार्थं, न अन्यथा।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: श्री भगवान् उवाच The Supreme Lord said, (हे) सख्यः O friends, ये मिथः भजन्ति those who love only for the sake of mutual reciprocation, ते हि स्वार्थैकान्तोद्यमाः they are indeed prompted solely by their own self-interest. तत्र सौहृदं धर्मः न In that case there is neither true friendship nor virtue, तत् हि स्वार्थार्थं for that is certainly only for the sake of self-gratification, न अन्यथा and nothing else.

Simple Meaning: The Supreme Lord said: O friends, those who reciprocate love only when they receive something in return are simply acting out of self-interest. In such dealings, there is no real friendship and no true virtue. It is essentially a business transaction aimed at one's own benefit and nothing more.

भजन्त्यभजतो ये वै करुणाः पितरो यथा ।

धर्मो निरपवादोऽत्र सौहृदं च सुमध्यमाः ॥10.32.18॥

Word-by-word meaning: भजन्ति show love/care, अभजतः to those who do not show love/reciprocate, ये those who, वै indeed, करुणाः merciful/kind, पितरः parents, यथा just as, धर्मः virtue/duty, निरपवादः faultless/pure, अत्र in this, सौहृदम् true friendship/affection, च and, सुमध्यमाः O slender-waisted girls

Anvaya: (हे) सुमध्यमाः! पितरः यथा, ये अभजतः अपि भजन्ति, अत्र वै निरपवादः धर्मः सौहृदं च (अस्ति)।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: (हे) सुमध्यमाः O slender-waisted ones, पितरः यथा just like parents, ये अभजतः अपि भजन्ति those who show love even to those who do not reciprocate, अत्र वै निरपवादः धर्मः in this case there is indeed faultless virtue, सौहृदं च and true affection.

Simple Meaning: O slender-waisted gopis, those who show love even to those who do not reciprocate are truly merciful, just like parents who love their children unconditionally. In such selfless behavior, there is pure, faultless virtue and genuine affection.

भजतोऽपि न वै केचिद् भजन्त्यभजतः कुतः ।

आत्मारामा ह्याप्तकामा अकृतज्ञा गुरुद्रुहः ॥10.32.19॥

Word-by-word meaning: भजतः those who are loving/devoted, अपि even, न not, वै certainly, केचित् some (persons), भजन्ति reciprocate, अभजतः to those who do not love, कुतः what to speak of/how, आत्मा-रामः those who take pleasure in the self, हि indeed, आप्त-कामाः those whose desires are already fulfilled, अकृत-ज्ञाः ungrateful, गुरु-द्रुहः treacherous toward their superiors/benefactors

Anvaya: केचित् भजतः अपि न भजन्ति, अभजतः कुतः? (ते चतुर्विधाः) — आत्मारामाः, आप्तकामाः, अकृतज्ञाः, गुरुद्रुहः हि।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: केचित् भजतः अपि न भजन्ति Some people do not reciprocate even toward those who love them, अभजतः कुतः so how would they ever love those who do not love them? (ते चतुर्विधाः) हि They are indeed of four types: आत्मारामाः those who find all satisfaction within themselves, आप्तकामाः those who have already attained all they desire, अकृतज्ञाः the ungrateful, गुरुद्रुहः and those who turn against their well-wishers.

Simple Meaning: Then there are those who do not reciprocate love even when it is offered to them, so there is no question of them loving those who don't love them. These people fall into four categories: those who are self-satisfied and need no one else; those whose every desire is already fulfilled; those who are simply ungrateful; and those who are inherently treacherous toward those who care for them.

नाहं तु सख्यो भजतोपि जन्तून् भजाम्यमीषामनुवृत्तिवृत्तये ।

यथाधनो लब्धधने विनष्टे तच्चिन्तयान्यन्निभृतो न वेद ॥10.32.20॥

Word-by-word meaning: न not, अहम् I, तु but, सख्यः O friends, भजतः those who are devoted, अपि even, जन्तून् to living beings, भजामि I reciprocate, अमीषाम् of them, अनुवृत्ति-वृत्तये for the sake of increasing their constant meditation (on Me), यथा just as, अधनः a poor man, लब्ध-धने when wealth he has acquired, विनष्टे is lost, तत्-चिन्तया by the constant thought of that (wealth), अन्यत् anything else, निभृतः absorbed, न वेद does not know

Anvaya: सख्यः! अहं तु भजतः अपि जन्तून् न भजामि, (तत्) अमीषाम् अनुवृत्तिवृत्तये (एव)। यथा लब्धधने विनष्टे अधनः तच्चिन्तया निभृतः अन्यत् न वेद (तथा)।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: सख्यः O friends, अहं तु भजतः अपि जन्तून् न भजामि I, however, do not always reciprocate with those devoted to Me, अमीषाम् अनुवृत्तिवृत्तये in order to increase their constant meditation on Me. यथा लब्धधने विनष्टे अधनः Just as when a poor man loses a treasure he has just found, तच्चिन्तया निभृतः he becomes so absorbed in the thought of it अन्यत् न वेद that he knows nothing else.

Simple Meaning: O friends, the reason I do not always immediately reciprocate the devotion of My devotees is that I want to increase their constant meditation upon Me. Just like a poor man who acquires a great treasure and then loses it; he becomes so intensely absorbed in thoughts of his lost wealth that he forgets everything else in the world. Similarly, by disappearing, I ensure that your hearts are entirely focused on Me.

एवं मदर्थोज्झितलोकवेदस्वानां हि वो मय्यनुवृत्तयेऽबलाः ।

मया परोक्षं भजता तिरोहितं मासूयितुं मार्हथ तत् प्रियं प्रियाः ॥10.32.21॥

Word-by-word meaning: एवम् in this way, मत्-अर्थ-उज्झित-लोक-वेद-स्वानाम् who for My sake have abandoned worldly customs, Vedic injunctions, and relatives, हि indeed, वः of you, मयि in Me, अनुवृत्तये for the sake of constant attachment/meditation, अबलाः O women (gopis), मया by Me, परोक्षम् out of sight, भजता showing favor (in an invisible way), तिरोहितम् disappeared, मा do not, असूयितुम् to be resentful/find fault, मा not, अर्हथ you should, तत् therefore, प्रियम् toward the beloved, प्रियाः O dear ones

Anvaya: (हे) अबलाः! एवं मदर्थोज्झितलोकवेदस्वानां वः मयि अनुवृत्तये हि परोक्षं भजता मया तिरोहितम्। (हे) प्रियाः! तत् प्रियं मा असूयितुम् अर्हथ।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: (हे) अबलाः O women, एवं मदर्थोज्झितलोकवेदस्वानां वः for you, who have thus abandoned the world, the Vedas, and your own kin for My sake, मयि अनुवृत्तये हि indeed to ensure your constant attachment to Me, परोक्षं भजता मया तिरोहितम् I disappeared while still favoring you invisibly. (हे) प्रियाः O dear ones, तत् प्रियं मा असूयितुम् अर्हथ therefore, you should not be resentful toward your beloved.

Simple Meaning: O My dear gopis, you have sacrificed everything—social reputation, religious duties, and even your families—just for Me. To ensure that your hearts remain eternally and intensely attached to Me, I disappeared from your sight. Even while hidden, I was actually reciprocating your love by intensifying your devotion. Therefore, My dear ones, please do not harbor any resentment toward Me, your beloved.

न पारयेऽहं निरवद्यसंयुजां स्वसाधुकृत्यं विबुधायुषापि वः ।

या माभजन् दुर्जरगेहशृङ्खलाः संवृश्च्य तद् वः प्रतियातु साधुना ॥10.32.22॥

Word-by-word meaning: न पारये I am not able, अहम् I, निरवद्य-संयुजाम् of those whose connection (with Me) is faultless/pure, स्व-साधु-कृत्यम् a proper return for your virtuous deed, विबुध-आयुषा with a lifetime of the Brahma (millions of years), अपि even, वः to you, याः who, मा Me, अभजन् have worshipped/joined, दुर्जर-गेह-शृङ्खलाः the difficult-to-break chains of household life, संवृश्च्य cutting asunder, तत् that (deed), वः of you, प्रतियातु let it be repaid, साधुना by (your own) virtuous nature/goodness

Anvaya: याः दुर्जरगेहशृङ्खलाः संवृश्च्य मा अभजन्, (तथा) निरवद्यसंयुजां वः स्वसाधुकृत्यं विबुधायुषा अपि अहं न पारये। तत् वः साधुना एव प्रतियातु।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: याः दुर्जरगेहशृङ्खलाः संवृश्च्य मा अभजन् You who have joined Me by completely cutting the unbreakable chains of domestic life, निरवद्यसंयुजां वः स्वसाधुकृत्यं for your faultless service and union, विबुधायुषा अपि अहं न पारये I am unable to repay you even in a lifetime of Brahma. तत् वः साधुना एव प्रतियातु Therefore, let that debt be repaid by your own glorious and saintly nature.

Simple Meaning: I am unable to repay My debt to you even if I were to try for the entire lifespan of Brahma. Your love and connection with Me are completely pure and beyond reproach. You have abandoned the nearly unbreakable bonds of family and home just to be with Me. Since I cannot possibly offer a fitting return for such sacrifice, let your own selfless love and virtue be your reward.

इति श्रीमद्भागवते महापुराणे पारमहंस्यां संहितायां दशमस्कन्धे पूर्वार्धे “रासक्रीडायां गोपीसान्त्वनं नाम” द्वात्रिंशः अध्यायः ॥32॥

Thus ends the thirty-second discourse named “The Lord comforts the Gopis during the Rasa Play”, in the first half of Book Ten of the great and glorious Bhagavata-Purana.
