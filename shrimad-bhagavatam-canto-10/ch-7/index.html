Deliverance of Trinavarta

श्रीमद्भागवतमहापुराणम्

दशमः स्कन्धः

तृणावर्तमोक्षः नाम सप्तमः अध्यायः

(37 Shlokas)

राजा उवाच

येन येनावतारेण भगवान् हरिरीश्वरः ।

करोति कर्णरम्याणि मनोज्ञानि च नः प्रभो ॥10.7.1॥

Word-by-word meaning: राजा उवाच the king said, येन येन by whichever, अवतारेण incarnation, भगवान् the Supreme Lord, हरिः Hari, ईश्वरः the controller, करोति performs, कर्ण-रम्याणि delightful to the ears, मनः-ज्ञानि pleasing to the mind, च and, नः our, प्रभो O master

Anvaya: राजा उवाच — प्रभो! भगवान् ईश्वरः हरिः येन येन अवतारेण नः कर्णरम्याणि मनोज्ञानि च करोति।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: राजा उवाच The king said — प्रभो O master, भगवान् ईश्वरः हरिः the Supreme Lord Hari, the controller, येन येन अवतारेण by whichever form he incarnates, नः कर्णरम्याणि मनोज्ञानि च which are delighting to our ears and pleasing to our minds, करोति (he) performs (deeds).

Simple meaning: The king said: O master, by whatever incarnation the Supreme Lord Hari, the controller, appears, He performs pastimes that are delightful to our ears and pleasing to our minds.

यच्छृण्वतोऽपैत्यरतिर्वितृष्णा सत्त्वं च शुद्ध्यत्यचिरेण पुंसः ।

भक्तिर्हरौ तत्पुरुषे च सख्यं तदेव हारं वद मन्यसे चेत् ॥10.7.2॥

Word-by-word meaning: यत् which, शृण्वतः of one hearing, अपैति goes away, अरतिः indifference, वितृष्णा detachment from worldly objects, सत्त्वम् existence/being/conscience, च and, शुद्ध्यति is purified, अचिरेण quickly, पुंसः of a person, भक्तिः devotion, हरौ in Hari, तत्-पुरुषे in His devotees, च and, सख्यम् friendship, तत् that, एव indeed, हारम् related to Hari, वद speak, मन्यसे you consider, चेत् if

हारम्   हारं , त्रि, (हरेरिदं । हरि + अण् ) हरिसम्बन्धीयम्  src. Kosha Ashtadhyayi

Anvaya: यत् शृण्वतः अरतिः वितृष्णा अपैति, पुंसः सत्त्वं च अचिरेण शुद्ध्यति, हरौ भक्तिः तत्पुरुषे च सख्यं, चेत् मन्यसे तत् एव हारं वद।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: यत् शृण्वतः अरतिः वितृष्णा अपैति By hearing which, indifference and worldly attachment go away, पुंसः सत्त्वं च अचिरेण शुद्ध्यति and a person's existence is quickly purified, हरौ भक्तिः तत्पुरुषे च सख्यं devotion to Hari and friendship with His devotees arise, चेत् मन्यसे if you consider (it fit), तत् एव हारं वद then speak of (the stories) related to Hari.

Simple meaning: Those pastimes which, upon hearing, remove indifference and worldly attachment, quickly purify a person's existence, and awaken devotion to Hari and friendship with His devotees—if you consider it fit, then please speak of those pastimes of Hari.

अथान्यदपि कृष्णस्य तोकाचरितमद्भुतम् ।

मानुषं लोकमासाद्य तज्जातिमनुरुन्धतः॥10.7.3॥

Word-by-word meaning: अथ now, अन्यत् another, अपि also, कृष्णस्य of Krishna, तोक-आचरितम् childhood pastimes, अद्भुतम् wonderful, मानुषम् human, लोकम् world, आसाद्य having come to, तत्-जातिम् that species/nature (human nature), अनुरुन्धतः imitating/conforming to

Anvaya: अथ मानुषं लोकम् आसाद्य तज्जातिम् अनुरुन्धतः कृष्णस्य अद्भुतम् अन्यत् तोकाचरितम् अपि (वद)।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: अथ Now, मानुषं लोकम् आसाद्य having come to the human world, तज्जातिम् अनुरुन्धतः conforming to that nature, कृष्णस्य of Krishna, अद्भुतम् अन्यत् तोकाचरितम् अपि वद another wonderful childhood pastime also, please tell.

Simple meaning: Now, another wonderful childhood pastime of Krishna, who having come to the human world, was acting in conformity with human nature, please tell.

श्री शुक उवाच

कदाचिदौत्थानिककौतुकाप्लवे जन्मर्क्षयोगे समवेतयोषिताम् ।

वादित्रगीतद्विजमन्त्रवाचकैश्चकार सूनोरभिषेचनं सती ॥10.7.4॥

Word-by-word meaning: श्री शुकः उवाच Sri Shuka said, कदाचित् once, औत्थानिक-कौतुक-आप्लवे on the festive bathing ceremony relating to the turning over (of a child), जन्म-ऋक्ष-योगे when the (same) constellation (Rohini) under which He was born happened to be at the ascendant, समावेत-योषिताम् with assembled women, वादित्र-गीत-द्विज-मन्त्र-वाचकैः with instrumental music songs and brahmins chanting mantras, चकार performed, सूनोः of her son, अभिषेचनम् bathing ceremony, सती the chaste lady

औत्थानिक  mfn. (fr. उत्-थान), relating to the getting up or sitting up (of a child), src. Kosha Ashtadhyayi

Anvaya: श्री शुकः उवाच — कदाचित् जन्मर्क्षयोगे औत्थानिककौतुकाप्लवे समावेतयोषितां वादित्रगीतद्विजमन्त्रवाचकैः सती सूनोः अभिषेचनं चकार।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: श्री शुकः उवाच Sri Shuka said — कदाचित् जन्मर्क्षयोगे Once when the (same) constellation (Rohini) under which He was born happened to be at the ascendant, औत्थानिककौतुकाप्लवे on the festive bathing ceremony relating to the getting up or sitting up, समावेतयोषितां with assembled women, वादित्रगीतद्विजमन्त्रवाचकैः with instrumental music, songs and brahmins chanting mantras, सती the chaste lady, सूनोः अभिषेचनं चकार performed the bathing ceremony of her son.

Simple meaning: Sri Shuka said: Once, on the auspicious occasion of the ceremonial bathing to celebrate the infant Krishna's first turning in bed—a rite typically performed at three months—when the constellation Rohini under which He was born had returned to ascendance, Yashoda performed the sacred ablution ceremony for her son. Surrounded by assembled ladies, the ritual was accompanied by the playing of musical instruments, festive songs, and the chanting of Vedic mantras by the brahmins.

नन्दस्य पत्नी कृतमज्जनादिकं विप्रैः कृतस्वस्त्ययनं सुपूजितैः ।

अन्नाद्यवासःस्रगभीष्टधेनुभिः संजात निद्राक्षमशीशयच्छनैः ॥10.7.5॥

Word-by-word meaning: नन्दस्य पत्नी Nanda's wife, कृत-मज्जन-आदिकम् having completed bathing and other rituals, विप्रैः by brahmins, कृत-स्वस्त्ययनम् for whom benedictions were performed, सु-पूजितैः well honored, अन्न-आदि-वासः-स्रक्-अभीष्ट-धेनुभिः with food, clothing, garlands, desired objects and cows, संजात-निद्रा-अक्षम् whose eyes became sleepy, अशीशयत् laid down, शनैः gently

Anvaya: नन्दस्य पत्नी कृतमज्जनादिकम्, अन्नाद्यवासःस्रगभीष्टधेनुभिः सुपूजितैः विप्रैः कृतस्वस्त्ययनं, संजातनिद्राक्षं शनैः अशीशयत्।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: नन्दस्य पत्नी Nanda's wife, कृतमज्जनादिकं (the child) for whom the bathing and other rituals were complete, अन्नाद्यवासःस्रगभीष्टधेनुभिः सुपूजितैः विप्रैः by the brahmins who were honored with food, clothing, garlands and desired objects and cows, कृतस्वस्त्ययनं (the child) for whom benedictions were performed, संजातनिद्राक्षं (the child) whose eyes had become sleepy, शनैः अशीशयत् gently laid down.

Simple meaning: After completing the bathing ceremony and other rituals for the child, Nanda's wife honored the brahmins who had performed the benedictions with gifts of food, clothing, garlands and desired objects and cows. Then she gently laid down the child whose eyes had grown sleepy.

औत्थानिकौत्सुक्यमना मनस्विनी समागतान् पूजयती व्रजौकसः ।

नैवाशृणोद् वै रुदितं सुतस्य सा रुदन् स्तनार्थी चरणावुदक्षिपत् ॥10.7.6॥

Word-by-word meaning: औत्थानिक-उत्सुक्य-मनाः mind absorbed in festive enthusiasm, मनस्विनी noble minded lady, समागतान् those who had assembled, पूजयती honoring, व्रज-ओकसः residents of Vraja, न not, एव indeed, अशृणोत् heard, वै indeed, रुदितम् crying, सुतस्य of her son, सा she, रुदन् crying, स्तन-अर्थी desiring breast, चरणौ feet, उदक्षिपत् kicked upward

Anvaya: औत्थानिकौत्सुक्यमना मनस्विनी समागतान् व्रजौकसः पूजयती सा सुतस्य रुदितं न एव अशृणोत् वै। स्तनार्थी रुदन् चरणौ उदक्षिपत्।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: औत्थानिकौत्सुक्यमना मनस्विनी The noble minded lady with mind absorbed in festive enthusiasm, समागतान् व्रजौकसः पूजयती honoring the assembled residents of Vraja, सा सुतस्य रुदितं न एव अशृणोत् वै she did not indeed hear her son's crying. स्तनार्थी रुदन् चरणौ उदक्षिपत् He, desiring the breast and crying, kicked His feet upward.

Simple meaning: The noble minded Yashoda, her mind absorbed in the festive celebration, was busy honoring the assembled residents of Vraja and did not hear her son crying. Meanwhile the child, desiring milk and crying, kicked His feet upward.

अधः शयानस्य शिशोरनोऽल्पकप्रवालमृद्वङ्घ्रिहतं व्यवर्तत ।

विध्वस्तनानारसकुप्यभाजनं व्यत्यस्तचक्राक्षविभिन्नकूबरम् ॥10.7.7॥

Word-by-word meaning: अधः below, शयानस्य lying, शिशोः of the child, अनः cart, अल्पक-प्रवाल-मृदु-अङ्घ्रि-हतम् struck by small and tender coral-colored soft feet, व्यवर्तत overturned, विध्वस्त-नाना-रस-कुप्य-भाजनम् with various liquids in earthen vessels scattered, व्यत्यस्त-चक्र-अक्ष-विभिन्न-कूबरम् with displaced wheels, axles and broken poles

Anvaya: अधः शयानस्य शिशोः अल्पकप्रवालमृद्वङ्घ्रिहतं विध्वस्तनानारसकुप्यभाजनं व्यत्यस्तचक्राक्षविभिन्नकूबरम् अनः व्यवर्तत।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: अधः शयानस्य शिशोः Of the child lying below, अल्पकप्रवालमृद्वङ्घ्रिहतं struck by small and tender coral-colored soft feet, विध्वस्तनानारसकुप्यभाजनं with various liquids and earthen vessels scattered, व्यत्यस्तचक्राक्षविभिन्नकूबरम् with wheels and axles displaced and poles broken, अनः the cart, व्यवर्तत overturned.

Simple meaning: The cart, struck by the tender coral-like soft feet of the child lying beneath it, overturned completely, scattering various liquids and earthen vessels everywhere, with its wheels and axles displaced and poles broken.

Note: The great rakshasa Hiranyaksha, who was slain by the Lord disguised as a boar (vide Chapters 17 to 19 of Book 3 of Srimad Bhagwatam), had a son, Utkacha by name. He was exceedingly powerful and well-built. In the course of his peregrinations he once crushed the trees of the hermitage of the sage Lomasha, who pronounced on him a curse that he would thenceforth remain disembodied. He felt that his physical sheath was about to fall off like slough. He fell prostrate at the feet of the sage and asked his forgiveness. Appeased by his penitent behaviour, the sage blessed him that in the course of the Vaivaswata Manvantara he would be liberated by the touch of the feet of Lord Shri Krishna. It was Utkacha that had entered the cart in Nanda’s residence in his disembodied state and was forthwith liberated by the touch of the Lord's feet.

दृष्ट्वा यशोदाप्रमुखा व्रजस्त्रिय औत्थानिके कर्मणि याः समागताः ।

नन्दादयश्चाद्भुतदर्शनाकुलाः कथं स्वयं वै शकटं विपर्यगात् ॥10.7.8॥

Word-by-word meaning: दृष्ट्वा having seen, यशोदा-प्रमुखाः Yashoda and others, व्रज-स्त्रियः women of Vraja, औत्थानिके in the ceremonial bathing, कर्मणि ritual, याः who, समागताः had assembled, नन्द-आदयः Nanda and others, च and, अद्भुत-दर्शन-आकुलाः bewildered by the wonderful sight, कथम् how, स्वयम् by itself, वै indeed, शकटम् cart, विपर्यगात् got overturned

Anvaya: औत्थानिके कर्मणि समागताः यशोदाप्रमुखाः व्रजस्त्रियः नन्दादयः च दृष्ट्वा अद्भुतदर्शनाकुलाः (अभवन्)। कथं वै शकटं स्वयं विपर्यगात्?

Literal meaning of the anvaya: औत्थानिके कर्मणि समागताः Having assembled for the ceremonial bathing ritual, यशोदाप्रमुखाः व्रजस्त्रियः नन्दादयः च Yashoda and other women of Vraja and Nanda and others, दृष्ट्वा अद्भुतदर्शनाकुलाः (अभवन्) having seen became bewildered by the wonderful sight कथं वै शकटं स्वयं विपर्यगात् How indeed did the cart overturn by itself?

Simple meaning: Yashoda and the other women of Vraja, along with Nanda and others who had assembled for the ceremonial bathing ritual, saw this and were bewildered by the wonderful sight. They wondered "How did this cart overturn by itself?"

ऊचुरव्यवसितमतीन् गोपान् गोपीश्च बालकाः ।

रुदतानेन पादेन क्षिप्तमेतन्न संशयः ॥10.7.9॥

Word-by-word meaning: ऊचुः said, अव्यवसित-मतीन् to those with uncertain minds, गोपान् cowherds, गोपीः cowherd women, च and, बालकाः children, रुदता by the crying one, अनेन by this, पादेन by foot, क्षिप्तम् kicked, एतत् this, न no, संशयः doubt

Anvaya: बालकाः अव्यवसितमतीन् गोपान् गोपीः च ऊचुः, रुदता अनेन पादेन एतत् क्षिप्तं, न संशयः।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: बालकाः The children, अव्यवसितमतीन् गोपान् गोपीः च ऊचुः said to the cowherds and cowherd women who were confused in their minds, रुदता अनेन पादेन एतत् क्षिप्तं this was kicked by the foot of this crying one, न संशयः there is no doubt.

Simple meaning: The children told the cowherds and cowherd women, who were uncertain about what had happened, "This cart was kicked over by the foot of this crying child—there is no doubt about it."

न ते श्रद्दधिरे गोपा बालभाषितमित्युत ।

अप्रमेयं बलं तस्य बालकस्य न ते विदुः ॥10.7.10॥

Word-by-word meaning: न not, ते they, श्रद्दधिरे believed, गोपाः cowherds, बाल-भाषितम् words of children, इति thus, उत indeed, अप्रमेयम् immeasurable, बलम् strength, तस्य of Him, बालकस्य of the child, न not, ते they, विदुः knew

Anvaya: ते गोपाः बालभाषितम् इति न श्रद्दधिरे। उत तस्य बालकस्य अप्रमेयं बलं न ते विदुः।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: ते गोपाः Those cowherds, बालभाषितम् इति considering it words of children, न श्रद्दधिरे did not believe. उत तस्य बालकस्य अप्रमेयं बलं न ते विदुः Indeed, they did not know the immeasurable strength of that child.

Simple meaning: Those cowherds, thinking these were merely the words of children, did not believe them. Indeed, they did not know of that child's immeasurable strength.

रुदन्तं सुतमादाय यशोदा ग्रहशङ्किता ।

कृतस्वस्त्ययनं विप्रै: सूक्तै: स्तनमपाययत् ॥10.7.11॥

Word-by-word meaning: रुदन्तम् crying, सुतम् son, आदाय taking, यशोदा Yashoda, ग्रह-शङ्किता fearing evil spirits, कृत-स्वस्त्ययनम् having performed benedictions, विप्रैः by brahmins, सूक्तैः with Vedic hymns, स्तनम् breast, अपाययत् gave to drink

Anvaya: यशोदा ग्रहशङ्किता विप्रैः सूक्तैः कृतस्वस्त्ययनं रुदन्तं सुतम् आदाय स्तनम् अपाययत्।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: यशोदा ग्रहशङ्किता Yashoda, fearing evil spirits, विप्रैः सूक्तैः कृतस्वस्त्ययनम् had the brahmins perform protective benedictions with Vedic hymns, रुदन्तं सुतम् आदाय taking her crying son, स्तनम् अपाययत् gave him her breast.

Simple meaning: Yashoda, fearing evil spirits, took up her crying son, had the brahmins perform protective benedictions with Vedic hymns, and then nursed him at her breast.

पूर्ववत् स्थापितं गोपैर्बलिभिः सपरिच्छदम् ।

विप्रा हुत्वार्चयाञ्चक्रुर्दध्यक्षतकुशाम्बुभिः ॥10.7.12॥

Word-by-word meaning: पूर्ववत् as before, स्थापितम् set up, गोपैः by the cowherds, बलिभिः strong ones, स-परिच्छदम् with utensils, विप्राः brahmins, हुत्वा having offered oblations, अर्चयाञ्चक्रुः worshiped, दधि-अक्षत-कुश-अम्बुभिः with yogurt, unbroken rice, kusha grass and water

Simple meaning: The strong cowherds set up the cart as before with all its utensils. The brahmins, having offered oblations in the fire, performed worship with yogurt, unbroken rice, kusha grass and water.

Word-by-word meaning: तृणावर्तः Trinavarta, शान्त-रयः whose strength had been destroyed, वात्या-रूप-धरः who had assumed a form of whirlwind, हरन् carrying, कृष्णम् Krishna, नभः-गतः gone to the sky, गन्तुम् to go, न not, अशक्नोत् was able, भूरि-भार-भृत् bearing immense weight

Anvaya: वात्यारूपधरः तृणावर्तः कृष्णं हरन् नभोगतः भूरिभारभृत् शान्तरयः (सन्) गन्तुं न अशक्नोत्।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: वात्यारूपधरः तृणावर्तः Trinavarta assuming the form of a whirlwind, कृष्णं हरन् carrying Krishna, नभोगतः having gone to the sky, भूरिभारभृत् bearing immense weight, शान्तरयः (सन्) with destroyed strength, गन्तुं न अशक्नोत् was not able to go further.

Simple meaning: Trinavarta, in the form of a whirlwind, having carried Krishna up to the sky, could not proceed further. Bearing Krishna's immense weight, his strength was destroyed.

तमश्मानं मन्यमान आत्मनो गुरुमत्तया ।

गले गृहीत उत्स्रष्टुं नाशक्नोदद्भुतार्भकम् ॥10.7.27॥

Word-by-word meaning: तम् Him, अश्मानम् stone, मन्यमानः thinking, आत्मनः of himself, गुरुमत्तया heavy, गले in throat, गृहीतः seized, उत्स्रष्टुम् to release, न not, अशक्नोत् was able, अद्भुत-अर्भकम् wonderful child

Anvaya: तम् अश्मानं मन्यमानः आत्मनः गुरुम् अत्तया गले गृहीतः अद्भुतार्भकम् उत्स्रष्टुं न अशक्नोत्।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: तम् अश्मानं मन्यमानः Thinking Him to be a stone, आत्मनः गुरुमत्तया heavier than himself, गले गृहीतः seized in the throat, अद्भुतार्भकम् उत्स्रष्टुं न अशक्नोत् was not able to release the wonderful child.

Simple meaning: Thinking the child to be as heavy as a stone, and finding Him too heavy for himself, the daitya, seized in the throat by Krishna's pressure, was unable to release the wonderful child.

गलग्रहणनिश्चेष्टो दैत्यो निर्गतलोचनः ।

अव्यक्तरावो न्यपतत् सहबालो व्यसुर्व्रजे ॥10.7.28॥

Word-by-word meaning: गल-ग्रहण-निश्चेष्टः motionless from throat seizure, दैत्यः daitya, निर्गत-लोचनः with eyes bulging out, अव्यक्त-रावः with indistinct sound, न्यपतत् fell down, सह-बालः with the child, व्यसुः lifeless, व्रजे in Vraja

Anvaya: गलग्रहणनिश्चेष्टः निर्गतलोचनः अव्यक्तरावः दैत्यः सहबालः व्यसुः व्रजे न्यपतत्।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: गलग्रहणनिश्चेष्टः Motionless from the throat seizure, निर्गतलोचनः with eyes bulging out, अव्यक्तरावः with indistinct sound, दैत्यः the daitya, सहबालः with the child, व्यसुः lifeless, व्रजे न्यपतत् fell down in Vraja.

Simple meaning: The daitya, rendered motionless from having his throat seized, with eyes bulging out and making indistinct sounds, fell down lifeless in Vraja along with the child.

तमन्तरिक्षात् पतितं शिलायां विशीर्णसर्वावयवं करालम् ।

पुरं यथा रुद्रशरेण विद्धं स्त्रियो रुदत्यो ददृशुः समेताः ॥107.29॥

Word-by-word meaning: तम् him, अन्तरिक्षात् from the sky, पतितम् fallen, शिलायाम् on the rock, विशीर्ण-सर्व-अवयवम् with all limbs shattered, करालम् terrible, पुरम् city, यथा as, रुद्र-शरेण by Rudra's arrow, विद्धम् pierced, स्त्रियः women, रुदत्यः crying, ददृशुः saw, समेताः assembled

Anvaya: समेताः रुदत्यः स्त्रियः अन्तरिक्षात् शिलायां पतितं विशीर्णसर्वावयवं करालं तं – रुद्रशरेण विद्धं पुरं यथा – ददृशुः।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: समेताः रुदत्यः स्त्रियः The assembled crying women, अन्तरिक्षात् शिलायां पतितम् fallen from the sky onto the rock, विशीर्णसर्वावयवं करालं with all limbs shattered and terrible appearance, तं him, रुद्रशरेण विद्धं पुरं यथा ददृशुः saw as if a city pierced by Rudra's arrow.

Simple meaning: The assembled crying women saw the daitya who had fallen from the sky onto a rock, with all his limbs shattered and of terrible appearance, like a city destroyed by Rudra's arrow.

प्रादाय मात्रे प्रतिहृत्य विस्मिताः कृष्णं च तस्योरसि लम्बमानम् ।

तं स्वस्तिमन्तं पुरुषादनीतं विहायसा मृत्युमुखात् प्रमुक्तम् ।

गोप्यश्च गोपाः किल नन्दमुख्या लब्ध्वा पुनः प्रापुरतीव मोदम् ॥10.7.30॥

Word-by-word meaning: प्रादाय having given, मात्रे to the mother, प्रतिहृत्य taking, विस्मिताः astonished, कृष्णम् Krishna, च and, तस्य his (on the daitya’s), उरसि on chest, लम्बमानम् hanging, तम् Him, स्वस्ति-मन्तम् safe and well, पुरुष-अद​-नीतम् who was taken away by the man-eater, विहायसा through the sky, मृत्यु-मुखात् from the mouth of death, प्रमुक्तम् released, गोप्यः gopis, च and, गोपाः cowherds, किल indeed, नन्द-मुख्याः led by Nanda, लब्ध्वा obtaining, पुनः again, प्रापुः attained, अतीव extreme, मोदम् joy

Anvaya: विस्मिताः तस्य उरसि लम्बमानं कृष्णं च प्रतिहृत्य मात्रे प्रादाय, विहायसा पुरुषात् अनीतं मृत्युमुखात् प्रमुक्तं तं स्वस्तिमन्तं पुनः लब्ध्वा नन्दमुख्याः गोपाः गोप्यः च किल अतीव मोदं प्रापुः।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: विस्मिताः Astonished, तस्य उरसि लम्बमानं कृष्णं च प्रतिहृत्य taking Krishna who hanging on his chest, मात्रे प्रादाय and giving Him to the mother, विहायसा through the sky, पुरुषात् अनीतं bought back by the daitya,  मृत्युमुखात् प्रमुक्तं released from the mouth of death, तं स्वस्तिमन्तं पुनः लब्ध्वा obtaining Him again safe and well, नन्दमुख्याः गोपाः गोप्यः च किल the cowherds and gopis led by Nanda indeed, अतीव मोदं प्रापुः attained extreme joy.

Simple meaning: Astonished, they took Krishna who was hanging on the daitya’s chest and gave Him to His mother. Having obtained Him again safe and well, after He had been carried through the sky by the daitya and released from the jaws of death, the cowherds and gopis led by Nanda experienced extreme joy.

अहो बतात्यद्भुतमेष रक्षसा बालो निवृत्तिं गमितोऽभ्यगात् पुनः ।

हिंस्रः स्वपापेन विहिंसितः खलः साधुः समत्वेन भयाद् विमुच्यते ॥10.7.31॥

Word-by-word meaning: अहो Oh, बत indeed, अति-अद्भुतम् very wonderful, एषः this, रक्षसा by the rakshasa, बालः child, निवृत्तिम् to death, गमितः taken, अभ्यगात् has returned, पुनः again, हिंस्रः violent one, स्व-पापेन by his own sin, विहिंसितः destroyed, खलः wicked, साधुः virtuous one, समत्वेन through equanimity, भयात् from fear, विमुच्यते is released

Anvaya: अहो बत अत्यद्भुतम्! रक्षसा निवृत्तिं गमितः एषः बालः पुनः अभ्यगात्। हिंस्रः खलः स्वपापेन विहिंसितः, साधुः समत्वेन भयात् विमुच्यते।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: अहो बत अत्यद्भुतम्! Oh, this is very wonderful indeed, रक्षसा निवृत्तिं गमितः carried to death by the rakshasa, एषः बालः this child, पुनः अभ्यगात् has returned again. हिंस्रः खलः स्वपापेन विहिंसितः The violent wicked one is destroyed by his own sin, साधुः समत्वेन भयात् विमुच्यते the virtuous one is released from fear through equanimity.

Simple meaning: Alas, this is truly very wonderful! This child who was carried to death by the rakshasa has returned again. The violent wicked one is destroyed by his own sin, while the virtuous one, through equanimity of mind, is released from all fear.

किं नस्तपश्चीर्णमधोक्षजार्चनं पूर्तेष्टदत्तमुत भूतसौहृदम् ।

यत्संपरेतः पुनरेव बालको दिष्ट्या स्वबन्धून् प्रणयन्नुपस्थितः ॥10.7.32॥

Word-by-word meaning: किम् what, नः our, तपः austerity, चीर्णम् performed, अधोक्षज-अर्चनम् worship of Adhokshaja (Lord Vishnu), पूर्त-इष्ट-दत्तम् public works sacrifices and charity, उत or, भूत-सौहृदम् compassion for beings, यत् by which, संपरेतः dead, पुनः again, एव indeed, बालकः child, दिष्ट्या by good fortune, स्व-बन्धून् own relatives, प्रणयन् delighting, उपस्थितः has returned

Anvaya: नः किं तपः चीर्णम्, अधोक्षजार्चनं, पूर्तेष्टदत्तम्, उत भूतसौहृदं, यत् दिष्ट्या संपरेतः बालकः स्वबन्धून् प्रणयन् पुनः एव उपस्थितः।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: नः किं तपः चीर्णम् What austerity have we performed, अधोक्षजार्चनं worship of Adhokshaja, पूर्तेष्टदत्तम् public works, sacrifices and charity, उत भूतसौहृदं or compassion for beings, यत् by which, दिष्ट्या by good fortune, संपरेतः बालकः the dead child, स्वबन्धून् प्रणयन् delighting his own relatives, पुनः एव उपस्थितः has indeed returned again.

Simple meaning: What austerity have we performed, what worship of the Lord Adhokshaja, what public works, sacrifices and charity, or what compassion for living beings have we shown, by which, through good fortune, this child who was dead has returned again, bringing delight to his relatives?

दृष्ट्वाद्भुतानि बहुशो नन्दगोपो बृहद्वने ।

वसुदेववचो भूयो मानयामास विस्मितः ॥10.7.33॥

Word-by-word meaning: दृष्ट्वा having seen, अद्भुतानि wonderful events, बहुशः many times, नन्द-गोपः Nanda the cowherd, बृहद्-वने in Brihadvana, वसुदेव-वचः Vasudeva's words, भूयः again, मानयामास honored/pondered/accepted how true they were, विस्मितः astonished

Anvaya: नन्दगोपः बृहद्वने बहुशः अद्भुतानि दृष्ट्वा विस्मितः वसुदेववचः भूयः मानयामास।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: नन्दगोपः Nanda the cowherd, बृहद्वने in Brihadvana, बहुशः अद्भुतानि दृष्ट्वा having seen many wonderful events, विस्मितः astonished, वसुदेववचः भूयः मानयामास accepted the truth of Vasudeva's words again.

Simple meaning: Having seen many wonderful events in Brihadvana, Nanda the cowherd was astonished and once again accepted the truth of the words that Vasudeva had spoken to him.

एकदार्भकमादाय स्वाङ्कमारोप्य भामिनि ।

प्रस्नुतं पाययामास स्तनं स्नेहपरिप्लुता ॥10.7.34॥

Word-by-word meaning: एकदा one day, अर्भकम् infant, आदाय taking, स्व-अङ्कम् own lap, आरोप्य placing upon, भामिनी lady, प्रस्नुतम् flowing with milk, पाययामास gave to drink, स्तनम् breast, स्नेह-परिप्लुता overwhelmed with affection

Anvaya: एकदा स्नेहपरिप्लुता भामिनी अर्भकम् आदाय स्वाङ्कम् आरोप्य प्रस्नुतं स्तनं पाययामास।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: एकदा One day, स्नेहपरिप्लुता भामिनी the lady overwhelmed with affection, अर्भकम् आदाय taking the infant, स्वाङ्कम् आरोप्य placing upon her own lap, प्रस्नुतं स्तनं पाययामास gave her breast flowing with milk to drink.

Simple meaning: One day, the lady overwhelmed with motherly affection, took the infant, placed Him on her lap, and nursed Him with her breast flowing with milk.

पीतप्रायस्य जननी सा तस्य रुचिरस्मितम् ।

मुखं लालयती राजञ्जृम्भतो ददृशे इदम् ॥10.7.35॥

Word-by-word meaning: पीत-प्रायस्य having mostly drunk, जननी mother, सा she, तस्य His, रुचिर-स्मितम् beautiful smiling, मुखम् face, लालयती caressing, राजन् O king, जृम्भतः of the yawning one, ददृशे saw, इदम् this

Anvaya: राजन्! पीतप्रायस्य जृम्भतः तस्य रुचिरस्मितं मुखं लालयती सा जननी इदं ददृशे।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: राजन् O king, पीतप्रायस्य जृम्भतः of Him who had mostly drunk and was yawning, तस्य रुचिरस्मितं मुखं His beautiful smiling face, लालयती caressing, सा जननी that mother, इदं ददृशे saw this.

Simple meaning: O king! As the mother was caressing the beautiful smiling face of her child who had mostly finished drinking and was yawning, she saw this (the following vision).

खं रोदसी ज्योतिरनीकमाशाः सूर्येन्दुवह्निश्वसनाम्बुधींश्च ।

द्वीपान् नगांस्तद्दुहितॄर्वनानि भूतानि यानि स्थिरजङ्गमानि ॥10.7.36॥

Word-by-word meaning: खम् sky, रोदसी heaven and earth, ज्योतिः-अनीकम् constellation of stars, आशाः directions, सूर्य-इन्दु-वह्नि-श्वसन-अम्बुधीन् sun, moon, fire, air and oceans, च and, द्वीपान् islands, नगान् mountains, तत्-दुहितॄः their daughters (rivers), वनानि forests, भूतानि beings, यानि which, स्थिर-जङ्गमानि stationary and moving

रोदसी - (भूम्याकाशयोः नाम, आकाशः, भूमिः) रोदस्,  सकारान्तः, नपुंसकलिङ्गः, असन्तः, द्वितीयाद्विवचनम् src. Shabdapath. Ashtadhyayi

दुहितॄः - दुहितृ,  ऋकारान्तः, स्त्रीलिङ्गः, द्वितीयाबहुवचनम् src. Shabdapath. Ashtadhyayi

Anvaya: खं रोदसी ज्योतिरनीकम् आशाः सूर्येन्दुवह्निश्वसनाम्बुधीन् च द्वीपान् नगान् तद्दुहितॄः वनानि यानि स्थिरजङ्गमानि भूतानि च।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: खं रोदसी The sky, heaven and earth, ज्योतिरनीकम् आशाः the luminaries and directions, सूर्येन्दुवह्निश्वसनाम्बुधीन् च the sun, moon, fire, air and oceans, द्वीपान् नगान् तद्दुहितॄः islands, mountains and their daughters (rivers), वनानि forests, यानि स्थिरजङ्गमानि भूतानि च which beings are stationary and moving.

Simple meaning: She saw the sky, heaven and earth, the luminaries and directions, the sun, moon, fire, air, and oceans, islands, mountains, rivers (daughters of mountains), forests, and all beings both stationary and moving.

सा वीक्ष्य विश्वं सहसा राजन् सञ्जातवेपथुः ।

सम्मील्य मृगशावाक्षी नेत्रे आसीत् सुविस्मिता ॥10.7.37॥

Word-by-word meaning: सा she, वीक्ष्य having seen, विश्वम् universe, सहसा suddenly, राजन् O king, सञ्जात-वेपथुः with trembling arisen, सम्मील्य closing, मृग-शाव-अक्षी the doe eyed one, नेत्रे eyes, आसीत् became, सु-विस्मिता greatly astonished

Anvaya: राजन्! सा विश्वं सहसा वीक्ष्य सञ्जातवेपथुः मृगशावाक्षी नेत्रे सम्मील्य सुविस्मिता आसीत्।

Literal meaning of the anvaya: राजन् O king, सा she, विश्वं सहसा वीक्ष्य having suddenly seen the universe, सञ्जातवेपथुः trembling arose in her, मृगशावाक्षी the doe eyed one, नेत्रे सम्मील्य closing her eyes, सुविस्मिता आसीत् became greatly astonished.

Simple meaning: O king! Having suddenly seen the entire universe within the child's mouth, she began to tremble. The doe-eyed Yashoda closed her eyes and became greatly astonished.

श्रीमद्भागवते महापुराणे पारमहंस्यां संहितायां दशमस्कन्धे पूर्वार्धे

तृणावर्तमोक्षः नाम सप्तमः अध्यायः ॥7॥

Thus ends the seventh discourse titled 'The deliverance of Trinavarta' in the great and glorious Bhagavata-Purana.
